</div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       Weighing approximately 14,288 kilograms (31,500\xc2\xa0lb),\n       <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       is the second of three node module on USOS.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-esa1_7-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-esa1-7">\n         [7]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The design is based on the existing\n       <a <a href="/wiki/Huntsville,_Alabama" title="Huntsville, Alabama">\n        Huntsville, Alabama\n       </a>\n       . The Space Station robotic arm,\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Canadarm2" title="Canadarm2">\n        Canadarm2\n       </a>\n       , is able to operate from a powered\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grapple_Fixture" title="Grapple Fixture">\n        grapple fixture\n       </a>\n       on the exterior of\n       <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NASA_10-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-NASA-10">\n         [10]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       After the cancellation of the\n       <a   </a>\n       </sup>\n       The first two were delivered on\n       <a href="/wiki/STS-126" title="STS-126">\n        STS-126\n       </a>\n       and the second two on\n       <a href="/wiki/STS-128" title="STS-128">\n        STS-128\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13">\n        <a href="#cite_note-13">\n         [13]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14">\n        <a href="#cite_note-14">\n         [14]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       The node measures 7.2 metres (24\xc2\xa0ft) in length, and it has a diameter of 4.4 metres (14\xc2\xa0ft).\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Construction_agreement">\n        Construction agreement\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a European Space Agency\n       </a>\n       the\n       <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">\n        Rome\n       </a>\n       -based company\n       <a href="/wiki/Thales_Alenia_Space" title="Thales </sup>\n       <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       arrived on June 1, 2003 at the\n       <a href="/wiki/Kennedy_Space_Center" title="Kennedy Space Center">\n        Kennedy Space Center\n Airbus Beluga\n       </a>\n       oversize cargo vehicle. Following post transportation inspection, the\n       <a href="/wiki/Italian_Space_Agency" title="Italian Space Agency">\n        Italian Space Agency\n       </a>\n       formally handed over\n       <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       to the\n       <a href="/wiki/European_Space_Agency" title="European Space Agency">\n        European Space Agency\n       </a>\n       (ESA). From there, ESA formally transferred ownership of\n       <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       to NASA on June 18, 2003, taking place in the\n       <a href="/wiki/Space_Station_Processing_Facility" title="Space Station Processing Facility">\n        Space Station Processing Facility\n       </a>\n       of the Kennedy Space Center.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-esa2_15-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-esa2-15">\n         [15]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The handover of\n       <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       completed a major element of the barter agreement, between ESA and NASA, that was signed in\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Turin,_Italy" title="Turin, Italy">\n        Turin\n       </a>\n       on October 8, 1997.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-esa2_15-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-esa2-15">\n         [15]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Paolo_A._Nespoli" title="Paolo A.    <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       module aboard\n       <a href="/wiki/STS-120" title="STS-120">\n        STS-120\n       </a>\n       as a mission specialist.\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n </a>\n       </sup>\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-manifest_18-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-manifest-18">\n         [18]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       On October 26, the station\'s\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Canadarm2" title="Canadarm2">\n        Space Station Remote Manipulator System\n       </a>\n       (SSRMS) removed\n       <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       from the shuttle cargo bay and temporarily mated it to the port side of\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Unity_Module" title="Unity Module">\n        Unity\n       </a>\n       and, on October 27, the crew entered\n       <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-harmmate_3-1">\n        <a </a>\n       </sup>\n       After the Space Shuttle departed\n       <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       was relocated to the forward dock of the\n       <i>\n        <a class="mw-redirect" The\n       <a href="/wiki/Expedition_16" title="Expedition 16">\n        Expedition 16\n       </a>\n       crew moved the\n       <a href="/wiki/Pressurized_Mating_Adapter" title="Pressurized Mating Adapter">\n        Pressurized Mating Adapter\n       </a>\n       (PMA-2) on November 12, 2007 from the\n       <i>\n        Destiny\n       </i>\n       Laboratory to the forward berth of\n       <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       .
