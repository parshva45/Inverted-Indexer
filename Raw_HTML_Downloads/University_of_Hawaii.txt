        edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       The\n       <a class="mw-redirect" class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/University_of_Hawai%CA%BBi" title="University of Hawai\xca\xbbi">\n        University of Hawai\xca\xbbi\n       </a>\n       system. class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=University_of_Hawaii&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Board of Regents">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       In accordance with Article X, Section 6 of the Hawai\xca\xbbi State Constitution, the University of Hawai\xca\xbbi system is governed by a Board of Regents, composed of 15 unpaid members who are nominated by a Regents Candidate Advisory Council, appointed by the governor, and confirmed by the state legislature. The Board oversees all aspects of governance for the university system, including its internal structure and management. The board also appoints, evaluates, and if necessary removes the President of the University of Hawai\xca\xbbi.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8">\n        <a href="#cite_note-8">\n         [8]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Student_regents">\n        Student regents\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=University_of_Hawaii&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Student regents">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <p>\n       The University\'s governing board includes a current student appointed by the Governor of Hawai\xca\xbbi to serve a two-year term as a full voting regent.
