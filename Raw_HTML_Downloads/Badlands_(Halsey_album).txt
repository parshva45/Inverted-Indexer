 </i>\n       is a\n       <a href="/wiki/Concept_album" title="Concept album">\n        concept album\n       </a>\n       that focuses on the fictional\n       <a href="/wiki/Dystopia" title="Dystopia">\n        dystopian\n       </a>\n       society known as The Badlands. The city is surrounded by a desert wasteland, keeping the inhabitants of The Badlands captive.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KROQ_9-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-KROQ-9">\n         [9]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The album was inspired by post-apocalyptic movies such as\n       <i>\n        <a href="/wiki/The_Fifth_Element" title="The Fifth Element">\n         The Fifth Element\n        </a>\n       </i>\n       . After writing the first few songs, Halsey came to the realization that the entire concept was a metaphor for her mental state. Halsey states that she created the Badlands as an escape from her real life struggles. In her opinion, the metaphor was that even with no escape, there is still optimism that there is somewhere else to go.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KROQ_9-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-KROQ-9">\n         [9]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The album was executive produced by\n       <a href="/wiki/Lido_(musician)" title="Lido (musician)">\n        Lido\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10">\n        <a href="#cite_note-10">\n         [10]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" April 7, 2015.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FMQB_12-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-FMQB-12">\n         [12]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Halsey announced "\n       <a
