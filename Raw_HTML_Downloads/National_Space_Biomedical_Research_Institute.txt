   </table>\n      <p>\n       The\n       <b>\n        National Space Biomedical Research Institute (NSBRI)\n       </b>\n       is a\n       <a href="/wiki/NASA" title="NASA">\n        NASA\n       </a>\n       -funded\n       <a href="/wiki/Consortium" title="Consortium">\n        consortium\n       </a>\n       of institutions studying the health risks related to long-duration\n       <a href="/wiki/Spaceflight" title="Spaceflight">\n        spaceflight\n       </a>\n       and developing solutions to reduce those risks. The NSBRI was founded in 1997. NSBRI\'s 16,400-square-foot headquarters facility is located in the\n       <a href="/wiki/BioScience_Research_Collaborative" title="BioScience Research Collaborative">\n        BioScience Research Collaborative\n       </a>\n       in\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Houston,_Texas" title="Houston, Texas">\n        Houston, Texas\n       </a>\n       . The Institute shares the facility with\n       <a href="/wiki/Baylor_College_of_Medicine" title="Baylor College of Medicine">\n        Baylor College of Medicine\n       </a>\n       \'s Center for Space Medicine. The official opening was held March 19, 2012.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Currently, NSBRI has seven teams conducting research at more than 60 institutions around the\n       <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">\n        United States\n       </a>\n       .\n       <a href="/wiki/NASA" title="NASA">\n        NASA\n       </a>\n       announced in March 2012 a five-year extension of its cooperative agreement with NSBRI.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mars-500" title="Mars-500">\n        Mars-500\n       </a>\n       Project\'s 520-day mission simulations with an experiment that monitored the six crew members\' rest-activity cycles, performance and psychological responses to determine the extent to which sleep loss, fatigue, stress, mood changes and conflicts occurred during the mission.\n      </p>\n Sutton and NSBRI Chairman of the Board of Directors Dr. Bobby R.
