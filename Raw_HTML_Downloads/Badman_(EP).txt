  <a href="#cite_note-pianist-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       "Coffee Shop", the EP\'s lead single, was written and produced by the\n       <a      </a>\n       </sup>\n       B.A.P filmed the music video in the city of\n       <a href="/wiki/Detroit" title="Detroit">\n        Detroit\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Michigan"
