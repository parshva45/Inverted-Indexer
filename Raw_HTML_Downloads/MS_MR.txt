"Fantasy". The music video for their debut single, "Hurricane", was released on April 26, 2012, with the single released on iTunes on July 10, 2012. It earned positive reviews for its "vintage" sound.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-theripe_4-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-theripe-4">\n         [4]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The single charted at number 38 in Germany.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-acharts_5-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-acharts-5">\n         [5]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       "Hurricane" was included on their debut EP,\n       <i>\n        <a href="/wiki/Candy_Bar_Creep_Show" title="Candy Bar Creep Show">\n         Candy Bar Creep Show\n        </a>\n       </i>\n       , alongside the songs "Bones", "Dark Doo Wop" and "Ash Tree Lane". The song was later released on iTunes on March 8, 2013 and became a single of the week.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Their debut album,\n       <i>\n        <a href="/wiki/Secondhand_Rapture" title="Secondhand Rapture">\n         Secondhand Rapture\n        </a>\n       </i>\n       , was released on May 10, 2013, and included all four songs from the prior EP.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       On July 17, 2015 they released their second album,\n       <i>\n        <a href="/wiki/How_Does_It_Feel_(MS_MR_album)" title="How Does It Feel (MS MR album)">\n         How Does It Feel\n        </a>\n       </i>\n       .
