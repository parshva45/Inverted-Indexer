 African easterly jet\n   </h1>\n   <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">\n    <div class="noprint" id="siteSub">\n     From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia\n    </div>\n    <div id="contentSub">\n    </div>\n    <div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">\n     Jump to:\n     <a href="#mw-head">\n      navigation\n     </a>\n     ,\n     <a href="#p-search">\n      search\n     </a>\n    </div>\n    <div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">\n     <div class="mw-parser-output">\n      <p>\n       The\n       <b>\n        African easterly jet\n       </b>\n       is a region of the lower troposphere over\n       <a href="/wiki/West_Africa" title="West Africa">\n        West Africa\n       </a>\n       where the seasonal mean wind speed is at a maximum and the wind is easterly. The temperature contrast between the\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sahara_Desert" title="Sahara Desert">\n        Sahara Desert\n       </a>\n       and the\n       <a href="/wiki/Gulf_of_Guinea" title="Gulf of Guinea">\n        Gulf of Guinea\n       </a>\n       causes the jet to form to the north of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Monsoon_trough" title="Monsoon trough">\n        monsoon trough\n       </a>\n       . The jet\'s maximum wind speeds are at a height of 3 kilometres (1.9\xc2\xa0mi). The jet moves northward from its south-most location in January, reaching its most northerly latitude in August. Its strongest winds are in September while it begins shifting back towards the equator. Within the easterly jet,\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_wave" title="Tropical wave">\n        tropical waves\n       </a>\n       form. Convective complexes associated with these waves can form\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclone" title="Tropical cyclone">\n        tropical cyclones\n       </a>\n       . If the jet is south of its normal location during August and September,\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclogenesis" title="Tropical cyclogenesis">\n        tropical cyclogenesis\n       </a>\n       is suppressed. If desertification continues across\n       <a href="/wiki/Sub-Saharan_Africa" title="Sub-Saharan Africa">\n        Sub-Saharan Africa\n       </a>\n       , the strength of the jet could increase, although tropical wave generation probably would decrease, which would decrease the number of tropical cyclones in the Atlantic basin.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n      </p>\n      <div class="toc" reading\n          </span>\n         </a>\n        </li>\n       </ul>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Formation_and_characteristics">\n        Formation and characteristics\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=African_easterly_jet&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Formation and characteristics">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       During January, the African easterly jet lies at 3,000 metres (9,800\xc2\xa0ft) above sea level at five degrees north latitude. Winds within it increase in speed from 30\xc2\xa0km/h (19\xc2\xa0mph) in January to 40\xc2\xa0km/h (25\xc2\xa0mph) in March. Shifting northward in April to the seventh parallel, winds within the jet increase to 45\xc2\xa0km/h (28\xc2\xa0mph). By June, it shifts northward into northwest Africa.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-marcel_1-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-marcel-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       From June into October, the\n       <a href="/wiki/Thermal_low" title="Thermal low">\n        thermal low\n       </a>\n       over northern Africa leads to a low-level westerly jet stream to the south of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Intertropical_Convergence_Zone" title="Intertropical Convergence Zone">\n        Intertropical Convergence Zone\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The mid-level African easterly jet develops because heating of the West African land mass during this time of year creates a surface temperature and moisture gradient between the Gulf of Guinea and the Sahara Desert. The atmosphere responds by generating vertical\n       <a href="/wiki/Wind_shear" title="Wind shear">\n        wind shear\n       </a>\n       to maintain\n       <a href="/wiki/Thermal_wind" title="Thermal wind">\n        thermal wind\n       </a>\n       balance. The jet reaches its zenith in August, lying between the 16th and 17th parallels. In September, winds maximize near 50\xc2\xa0km/h (31\xc2\xa0mph) between the 12th and 13th parallels. The easterly jet weakens and drops southward during October and November.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-marcel_1-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-marcel-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Impact">\n        Impact\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=African_easterly_jet&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Impact">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">\n       See also:\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclones_and_climate_change#Hurricanes" title="Tropical cyclones and climate change">\n        Tropical cyclones and climate change \xc2\xa7\xc2\xa0Hurricanes\n       </a>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       The mid-level African easterly jet is considered to play a crucial role in the southwest\n       <a href="/wiki/Monsoon" title="Monsoon">\n        monsoon\n       </a>\n       of Africa,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and helps form the tropical waves that move across the tropical Atlantic and eastern Pacific oceans during the warm season.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">\n        <a href="#cite_note-4">\n         [4]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The jet exhibits both\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Barotropic" title="Barotropic">\n        barotropic\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Baroclinic_instability" title="Baroclinic instability">\n        baroclinic instability\n       </a>\n       , which produces\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Synoptic_scale" title="Synoptic scale">\n        synoptic scale\n       </a>\n       , westward-propagating disturbances in the jet known as African easterly waves or tropical waves. A small number of mesoscale storm systems embedded in these waves develop into tropical cyclones after they move from west Africa into the tropical Atlantic, mainly during August and September. When the jet is south of normal during the peak months of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_hurricane_season" title="Atlantic hurricane season">\n        Atlantic hurricane season\n       </a>\n       , tropical cyclone formation is suppressed.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5">\n        <a href="#cite_note-5">\n         [5]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="See_also">\n        See also\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=African_easterly_jet&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: See also">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <ul>\n       <li>\n        <a href="/wiki/Saharan_Air_Layer" title="Saharan Air Layer">\n         Saharan Air Layer\n        </a>\n       </li>\n      </ul>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="References">\n        References\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=African_easterly_jet&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: References">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <div class="reflist columns references-column-width" style="-moz-column-width: 30em;
