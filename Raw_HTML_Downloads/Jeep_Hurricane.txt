The Hurricane doesn\xe2\x80\x99t have side doors or a roof, and there is only seating for two people. The driver and passenger enter the vehicle over bulkheads on each side. The Hurricane features a Chrysler designed and patented four-wheel steering system, which was outsourced to\n       <a href="/wiki/MillenWorks" title="MillenWorks">\n        MillenWorks\n       </a>\n       that allows the Hurricane to move sideways.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Jeep_4-wheel_steering_2-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-Jeep_4-wheel_steering-2">\n         [2]\n
