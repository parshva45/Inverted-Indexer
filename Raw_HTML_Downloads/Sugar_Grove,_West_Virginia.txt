small community located in\n       <a href="/wiki/Pendleton_County,_West_Virginia" title="Pendleton County, West Virginia">\n        Pendleton County\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/West_Virginia" title="West Virginia">\n        West Virginia\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-1">\n         [1]\n Its\n       <a href="/wiki/ZIP_Code" title="ZIP Code">\n        ZIP Code\n       </a>\n       is 26815. It is located within the\n       <a href="/wiki/United_States_National_Radio_Quiet_Zone" title="United States National Radio Quiet Zone">\n        United States National Radio Quiet Zone\n       </a>\n       .\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       The community was named for a sugar orchard at the original town site.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The\n       <a href="/wiki/Bowers_House_(Sugar_Grove,_West_Virginia)" title="Bowers House (Sugar Grove, West Virginia)">\n        Bowers House\n       </a>\n       was listed on the\n       <a href="/wiki/National_Register_of_Historic_Places" title="National Register of Historic Places">\n        National Register of Historic Places\n       </a>\n       in 1985.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nris_3-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-nris-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       The nearby\n       <a href="/wiki/Sugar_Grove_Station" title="Sugar Grove Station">\n        Sugar Grove Station\n       </a>\n       is operated by the\n       <a href="/wiki/National_Security_Agency" title="National Security Agency">\n        National Security Agency\n       </a>\n       .\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       In 2017, the Navy Operations Information Command base in Sugar Grove was auctioned off by the\n       <a href="/wiki/General_Services_Administration" title="General Services Administration">\n        General Services Administration\n       </a>\n       .
