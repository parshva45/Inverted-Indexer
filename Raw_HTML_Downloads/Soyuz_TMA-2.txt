class="mw-headline" id="Mission_highlights">\n        Mission highlights\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Soyuz_TMA-2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Mission highlights">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       Originally the Soyuz missions to the ISS were all planned to be only taxi mission to deliver a new Soyuz spacecraft as the station\'s lifeboat every six month with a visiting crew, but not for crew exchange. Until the\n       <a href="/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Columbia_disaster" title="Space Shuttle Columbia disaster">\n        Space Shuttle Columbia disaster\n       </a>\n       the same was planned for Soyuz TMA-2, a visiting crew consisting of commander\n       <a href="/wiki/Gennady_Padalka" title="Gennady Padalka">\n        Gennady Padalka\n       </a>\n       and ESA-astronaut\n       <a href="/wiki/Pedro_Duque" title="Pedro Duque">\n        Pedro Duque\n       </a>\n       were to spend about one week at the station and then return with the previous\n       <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-1" title="Soyuz TMA-1">\n        Soyuz TMA-1\n       </a>\n       spacecraft. The third seat might have gone to the\n       <a href="/wiki/Chile" title="Chile">\n        Chilean\n       </a>\n       <a href="/wiki/Klaus_von_Storch" title="Klaus von Storch">\n        Klaus von Storch\n       </a>\n       as a Chilean space agency (Agencia Chilena del Espacio) cosmonaut, but even before the Columbia disaster it looked like his flight would not happen, and the seat would go to the Russian\n       <a href="/wiki/Astronaut" title="Astronaut">\n        cosmonaut\n       </a>\n       <a href="/wiki/Oleg_Kotov" title="Oleg Kotov">\n        Oleg Kotov\n       </a>\n       or to deliver freight to the station.\n      </p>\n      <div class="thumb tleft">\n       <div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;">\n        <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Soyuz_TMA-2_after_landing.jpg">\n         <img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1853" data-file-width="2870" height="142" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9a/Soyuz_TMA-2_after_landing.jpg/220px-Soyuz_TMA-2_after_landing.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9a/Soyuz_TMA-2_after_landing.jpg/330px-Soyuz_TMA-2_after_landing.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9a/Soyuz_TMA-2_after_landing.jpg/440px-Soyuz_TMA-2_after_landing.jpg 2x" width="220"/>\n        </a>\n        <div class="thumbcaption">\n         <div class="magnify">\n          <a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Soyuz_TMA-2_after_landing.jpg" title="Enlarge">\n          </a>\n         </div>\n         Soyuz TMA-2 landing\n        </div>\n       </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       During his stay on the station, Malenchenko became the first person to get married in space. His bride was in\n       <a href="/wiki/Texas" title="Texas">\n        Texas\n       </a>\n       where long distance marriages are legal.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       The spacecraft returned to Earth on October 28, with both the "Expedition 7" crew as well as\n       <a href="/wiki/Pedro_Duque" title="Pedro Duque">\n        Pedro Duque\n       </a>\n       on board. Duque was launched with Soyuz TMA-3 and spent only one week on board of the ISS.\n      </p>\n      <div style="clear:both;">\n      </div>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="References">\n        References\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Soyuz_TMA-2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: References">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">\n       <div class="mw-references-wrap">\n        <ol class="references">\n         <li id="cite_note-1">\n          <span class="mw-cite-backlink">\n           <b>\n            <a href="#cite_ref-1">\n             ^\n            </a>\n           </b>\n          </span>\n          <span class="reference-text">\n           <cite class="citation web">\n            <a class="external text" href="http://spaceflight.nasa.gov/station/crew/exp7/index.html" rel="nofollow">\n             "ISS Expedition 7 Crew"\n            </a>\n            .
