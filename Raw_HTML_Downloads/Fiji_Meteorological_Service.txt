<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">\n     <div class="mw-parser-output">\n      <div class="thumb tright">\n       <div class="thumbinner" style="width:148px;">\n        <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:-174_-20_globe.jpg">\n         <img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="146" data-file-width="146" height="146" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/b/b4/-174_-20_globe.jpg" width="146"/>\n        </a>\n        <div class="thumbcaption">\n         <div class="magnify">\n          <a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:-174_-20_globe.jpg" title="Enlarge">\n          </a>\n         </div>\n         Location of Fiji in the Pacific Ocean\n        </div>\n       </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       The\n       <b>\n        Fiji Meteorological Service (FMS)\n       </b>\n       is a Department of the government of\n       <a href="/wiki/Fiji" title="Fiji">\n        Fiji\n       </a>\n       responsible for providing weather forecasts and is based in\n       <a href="/wiki/Nadi" title="Nadi">\n        Nadi\n       </a>\n       . The current director of Fiji Meteorological Service is Ravind kumar. Since 1985, FMS has been responsible for naming and tracking\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclone" title="Tropical cyclone">\n        tropical cyclones\n       </a>\n       in the Southwest Pacific region. Current Meteorologists working at FMS have a Graduate Diploma in Meteorology from the Australian\n       <a href="/wiki/Bureau_of_Meteorology" title="Bureau of Meteorology">\n        Bureau of Meteorology\n       </a>\n       .\n      </p>\n      <p>\n      </p>\n      <div class="toc" id="toc">\n       <div class="toctitle">\n        <h2>\n         Contents\n        </h2>\n       </div>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Fiji_Meteorological_Service&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       In the 1910s, weather services for Fiji were considered part of the responsibility of the Harbour Board in Suva.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Role_of_Fiji_Meteorological_Services">\n        Role of Fiji Meteorological Services\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Fiji_Meteorological_Service&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Role of Fiji Meteorological Services">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <div class="floatright">\n       <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Nadi_town.jpg">\n        <img alt="Nadi town.jpg" data-file-height="922" data-file-width="1229" height="143" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/Nadi_town.jpg/190px-Nadi_town.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/Nadi_town.jpg/285px-Nadi_town.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/Nadi_town.jpg/380px-Nadi_town.jpg 2x" width="190"/>\n       </a>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       Since June 1995, the central weather office of Fiji, Nadi, has been one of six\n       <a href="/wiki/Regional_Specialized_Meteorological_Center" title="Regional Specialized Meteorological Center">\n        Regional Specialized Meteorological Centers\n       </a>\n       within the World Weather Watch program of the\n       <a href="/wiki/World_Meteorological_Organization" title="World Meteorological Organization">\n        World Meteorological Organization\n       </a>\n       . Its specialty is forecasting tropical cyclones south of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Equator" title="Equator">\n        equator\n       </a>\n       to the\n       <a href="/wiki/25th_parallel_south" title="25th parallel south">\n        25th parallel south\n       </a>\n       , and between the\n       <a href="/wiki/160th_meridian_east" title="160th meridian east">\n        160th meridian east\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/120th_meridian_west" title="120th meridian west">\n        120th meridian west\n       </a>\n       <a href="/wiki/Longitude" title="Longitude">\n        longitude\n       </a>\n       . FMS issues public and marine weather bulletins for\n       <a href="/wiki/Kiribati" title="Kiribati">\n        Kiribati\n       </a>\n       , Northern Cooks, Southern Cooks,\n       <a href="/wiki/Tuvalu" title="Tuvalu">\n        Tuvalu\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Tokelau" title="Tokelau">\n        Tokelau\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Niue" title="Niue">\n        Niue\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Nauru" title="Nauru">\n        Nauru\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/Fiji" title="Fiji">\n        Fiji\n       </a>\n       . The Fiji Meteorological Services, as per agreement with the International Civil Aviation Organization, functions as the Meteorological Watch Office for the Nadi Flight Information Region (FIR), which extends from Western\n       <a href="/wiki/Kiribati" title="Kiribati">\n        Kiribati\n       </a>\n       to\n       <a href="/wiki/Tuvalu" title="Tuvalu">\n        Tuvalu\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Fiji" title="Fiji">\n        Fiji\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Vanuatu" title="Vanuatu">\n        Vanuatu\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Wallis_and_Futuna" title="Wallis and Futuna">\n        Wallis and Futuna\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/New_Caledonia" title="New Caledonia">\n        New Caledonia\n       </a>\n       . However, it still provides certain aviation forecast services to Cook Islands, Christmas Island (Line Islands),\n       <a href="/wiki/Samoa" title="Samoa">\n        Samoa\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Niue" title="Niue">\n        Niue\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/Tonga" title="Tonga">\n        Tonga\n       </a>\n       which are situated outside the Nadi FIR boundary. Aviation products issued by FMS include\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Terminal_Aerodrome_Forecast" title="Terminal Aerodrome Forecast">\n        Terminal Aerodrome Forecasts\n       </a>\n       (TAF),\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Trend_Type_Forecast" title="Trend Type Forecast">\n        Trend Type Forecasts\n       </a>\n       (TTF), Area Forecasts, Route Forecasts, Inclement Weather Warnings for Nadi and Nausori Airports, Sigmets and Tropical Cyclone Advisories (TCA). Routine 24 hour TAF\'s are issued for Nadi (NFFN), Nausori (NFNA), Rarotonga (NCRG), Fuamotu (NFTF), Vavau (NFTV), Faleolo (NFSA), Niue, Tarawa (NGTA), and Christmas Island (PLCH) airports. 24 hour TAF\'s are also issued for Funafuti (NGFU) airport on certain days. TAF\'s (validity periods of less than 24 hours) are also issued for Aitutaki (NCAI), Manihiki (NCMH), Penrhyn (NCPY), Haapai (NFTL), Labasa (NFNL) and Rotuma (NFNR) airports.\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Tropical_Cyclone_Products_Issued_by_FMS">\n        Tropical Cyclone Products Issued by FMS\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Fiji_Meteorological_Service&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Tropical Cyclone Bulletins (SWB\'s), Tropical Disturbance Advisories (TDA\'s), Special Advisory for\n       <a href="/wiki/Samoa" title="Samoa">\n        Samoa\n       </a>\n       , Tropical Cyclone Advisories (TCA\'s), Tropical Cyclone Sigmets, CREX bulletins, International Marine Warnings associated with tropical cyclones (WTPS and WHPS) and Tropical cyclone forecast and uncertainty tracks are the tropical cyclone products issued by FMS.\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="See_also">\n        See also\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span
