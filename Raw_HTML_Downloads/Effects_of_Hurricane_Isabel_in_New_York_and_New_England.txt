Isabel in New York and New England\n       </b>\n       were relatively minor and primarily limited to wind damage.\n       <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Isabel" title="Hurricane Isabel">\n        Hurricane Isabel\n       </a>\n       formed from a\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_wave" title="Tropical wave">\n        tropical wave\n       </a>\n       on September 6, 2003 in the tropical\n       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">\n        Atlantic Ocean\n       </a>\n       . It moved northwestward, and within an environment of light wind shear and warm waters it steadily strengthened to reach peak winds of 165\xc2\xa0mph (265\xc2\xa0km/h) on September 11. After fluctuating in intensity for four days, Isabel gradually weakened and made landfall on the\n       <a href="/wiki/Outer_Banks" title="Outer Banks">\n        Outer Banks\n       </a>\n       of\n       <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina" title="North Carolina">\n        North Carolina\n       </a>\n       with winds of 105\xc2\xa0mph (165\xc2\xa0km/h) on September 18. It quickly weakened over land and became\n       <a href="/wiki/Extratropical_cyclone" title="Extratropical cyclone">\n        extratropical\n       </a>\n       over western\n       <a href="/wiki/Pennsylvania" title="Pennsylvania">\n        Pennsylvania\n       </a>\n       the next day.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Damage in\n       <a href="/wiki/New_York_(state)" title="New York (state)">\n        New York\n       </a>\n       totaled $90\xc2\xa0million (2003\xc2\xa0USD, $105\xc2\xa0million 2008\xc2\xa0USD),\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tcr_1-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-tcr-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       with\n       <a href="/wiki/Vermont" title="Vermont">\n        Vermont\n       </a>\n       reporting about $100,000 in damage (2003\xc2\xa0USD, $117,000 in 2008\xc2\xa0USD). Falling trees from moderate winds downed power lines across the region, causing sporadic power outages. Two people died as a result of the hurricane, both due to the rough surf from Isabel.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n      </p>\n      <div class="toc" id="toc">\n       <div class="toctitle">\n href="/w/index.php?title=Effects_of_Hurricane_Isabel_in_New_York_and_New_England&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Preparations">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       Hours before Isabel made landfall on North Carolina, the\n       <a href="/wiki/National_Hurricane_Center" title="National Hurricane Center">\n        National Hurricane Center\n       </a>\n       issued a\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclone_warnings_and_watches" Island\n       </a>\n       coastline from the\n       <a href="/wiki/New_Jersey" title="New Jersey">\n        New Jersey\n       </a>\n       /New York border to\n       <a href="/wiki/Moriches,_New_York" title="Moriches, New York">\n        Moriches\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tcr_1-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-tcr-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       About a day before the hurricane moved ashore, forecasters estimated the hurricane possessed a 10% chance of passing within 75\xc2\xa0miles (120\xc2\xa0km) of\n       <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">\n        New York City\n       </a>\n       . Other portions of southern\n       <a href="/wiki/New_England" title="New England">\n        New England\n       </a>\n       were estimated to have similar probabilities, with the same forecast predicting a 3% chance for it passing within 75\xc2\xa0miles (120\xc2\xa0km) of\n       <a href="/wiki/Eastport,_Maine" title="Eastport, Maine">\n        Eastport, Maine\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-prob45_2-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-prob45-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       By about two days before Isabel struck land until several hours after it moved ashore, forecasters predicted the storm to pass over western New York as a transitioning tropical cyclone before passing into\n       <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">\n        Canada\n       </a>\n       as an extratropical cyclone.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-archive_3-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-archive-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       <a href="/wiki/List_of_Governors_of_New_York" title="List of Governors of New York">\n        New York Governor\n       </a>\n       <a href="/wiki/George_Pataki" title="George Pataki">\n        George Pataki\n       </a>\n       urged residents to purchase emergency supplies and to fill cars with gasoline. The State Emergency Management Office began preparing for the hurricane about a week before it moved ashore. The office also issued a Level 1 emergency activation, with a planning unit readying contingency plans and in coordinating the efforts of other state offices. The state\'s\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/United_States_National_Guard" title="United States National Guard">\n        National Guard\n       </a>\n       began preliminary preparations for possible support efforts by reviewing the list of personnel able to be mobilized in the event of an emergency. Army and Air National Guard officials identified needed equipment in the event of an emergency, such as helicopters, generators, high-axle vehicles, and communications equipment. State police officers established contingency plans for personnel and equipment to assist as needed. The State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation ensured needed equipment were operational, and also secured buildings with sandbags to prevent flooding.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nysdot_4-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-nysdot-4">\n         [4]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Seven airlines allowed travelers potentially affected by the hurricane to reschedule their flights to a later date.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-airtravel_5-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-airtravel-5">\n         [5]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Impact">\n        Impact\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Effects_of_Hurricane_Isabel_in_New_York_and_New_England&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Impact">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <div class="thumb tright">\n       <div class="thumbinner" style="width:252px;">\n        <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Isabel_2003_rainfall.gif">\n         <img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="878" data-file-width="992" height="221" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/92/Isabel_2003_rainfall.gif/250px-Isabel_2003_rainfall.gif" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/92/Isabel_2003_rainfall.gif/375px-Isabel_2003_rainfall.gif 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/92/Isabel_2003_rainfall.gif/500px-Isabel_2003_rainfall.gif 2x" width="250"/>\n        </a>\n        <div class="thumbcaption">\n         <div class="magnify">\n          <a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Isabel_2003_rainfall.gif" title="Enlarge">\n          </a>\n         </div>\n         Rainfall totals from Hurricane Isabel\n        </div>\n       </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       The\n       <a href="/wiki/Pressure_gradient" title="Pressure gradient">\n        pressure gradient\n       </a>\n       between a\n       <a href="/wiki/Ridge_(meteorology)" title="Ridge (meteorology)">\n        ridge\n       </a>\n       and Isabel produced strong northeast winds of at least tropical storm force across southeastern New York.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nycncdc_6-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-nycncdc-6">\n         [6]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       <a href="/wiki/Shinnecock_Inlet" title="Shinnecock Inlet">\n        Shinnecock Inlet\n       </a>\n       recorded a peak gust of 52\xc2\xa0mph (84\xc2\xa0km/h).\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tcr_1-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-tcr-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       A station at\n       <a href="/wiki/LaGuardia_Airport" title="LaGuardia Airport">\n        LaGuardia Airport\n       </a>\n       recorded a wind gust of 51\xc2\xa0mph (82\xc2\xa0km/h),\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nycncdc_6-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-nycncdc-6">\n         [6]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       where airplane flights averaged a 90-minute delay.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cnn918_7-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-cnn918-7">\n         [7]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The strongest winds occurred in the outer\n       <a href="/wiki/Rainband" title="Rainband">\n        rainbands\n       </a>\n       , during which many trees, tree limbs, and power lines fell across the region. In the\n       <a href="/wiki/New_York_metropolitan_area" title="New York metropolitan area">\n        New York metropolitan area\n       </a>\n       , moderate winds downed 640\xc2\xa0trees and 801\xc2\xa0tree limbs. A man driving through\n       <a href="/wiki/Great_Neck_Estates,_New_York" title="Great Neck Estates, New York">\n        Great Neck Estates\n       </a>\n       was critically injured when a tree limb fell onto the car. Additionally, a falling tree branch hit a man in the head, resulting in a serious head injury.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nycncdc_6-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-nycncdc-6">\n         [6]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       In and around New York City, about 1.1\xc2\xa0million customers were left without power, though most outages were fixed by the day after the hurricane passed through the region.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-market_8-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-market-8">\n         [8]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Offshore of\n       <a href="/wiki/Long_Beach,_New_York" title="Long Beach, New York">\n        Long Beach\n       </a>\n       , rough waves killed a man while bodysurfing.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nycncdc_6-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-nycncdc-6">\n         [6]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Further to the northwest the winds were not as severe, though in some places in the south-central portion of the state the winds downed some trees. A falling tree hit and damaged a car in\n       <a href="/wiki/Cobleskill_(town),_New_York" title="Cobleskill (town), New York">\n        Cobleskill\n       </a>\n       . The winds also knocked out power to about 3,000\xc2\xa0customers in the\n       <a href="/wiki/Schoharie_Creek" title="Schoharie Creek">\n        Schoharie\n       </a>\n       and eastern\n       <a href="/wiki/Mohawk_Valley" title="Mohawk Valley">\n        Mohawk Valleys\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-snyncdc_9-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-snyncdc-9">\n         [9]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       In the northeast portion of the state, a few trees and power lines were blown down in\n       <a href="/wiki/Moriah,_New_York" title="Moriah, New York">\n        Moriah\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Westport,_New_York" title="Westport, New York">\n        Westport\n       </a>\n       , and\n       <a href="/wiki/Lewis,_Essex_County,_New_York" title="Lewis, Essex County, New York">\n        Lewis\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nenyncdc_10-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-nenyncdc-10">\n         [10]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Precipitation was generally light in the state, with a few scattered locations reporting over 1\xc2\xa0inch (25\xc2\xa0mm).\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rain_11-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-rain-11">\n         [11]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       In\n       <a href="/wiki/Livingston_County,_New_York" title="Livingston County, New York">\n        Livingston County\n       </a>\n       , the rainfall overflowed the reservoir, causing moderate flood damage.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ap919_12-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-ap919-12">\n         [12]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Initially, gasoline futures on the\n       <a href="/wiki/New_York_Stock_Exchange" title="New York Stock Exchange">\n        New York Stock Exchange\n       </a>\n       rose in anticipation of a threat from the hurricane, though later dropped when the storm failed to disrupt oil refining facilities.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nyt_13-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-nyt-13">\n         [13]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The hurricane brought unusual birds to the western portion of the state, including\n       <a href="/wiki/Petrel" title="Petrel">\n        petrels\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/Shearwater" title="Shearwater">\n        shearwaters\n       </a>\n       normally found in salt-water regions or over the open ocean. Most of the birds died within a few days due to the sudden change in habitat.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-bird_14-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-bird-14">\n         [14]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       In\n       <a href="/wiki/Cayuga_County,_New_York" title="Cayuga County, New York">\n        Cayuga County\n       </a>\n       , downed power lines lit one building on fire, causing minor smoke and water damage though no injuries.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ps920_15-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-ps920-15">\n         [15]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Damage in the state totaled $90\xc2\xa0million (2003\xc2\xa0USD, $105\xc2\xa0million 2008\xc2\xa0USD).\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tcr_1-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-tcr-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Rainfall reached 1\xc2\xa0inch (25\xc2\xa0mm) in portions of western\n       <a href="/wiki/Connecticut" title="Connecticut">\n        Connecticut\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/Massachusetts" title="Massachusetts">\n        Massachusetts\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rain_11-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-rain-11">\n         [11]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Strong surf from Isabel caused a man to drown after losing his footing along the beach near\n       <a href="/wiki/Narragansett,_Rhode_Island" title="Narragansett, Rhode Island">\n        Narragansett, Rhode Island\n       </a>\n       , and being swept out to sea.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rincdc_16-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-rincdc-16">\n         [16]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Officials in Connecticut contacted a water company in\n       <a href="/wiki/Watertown,_Connecticut" title="Watertown, Connecticut">\n        Watertown\n       </a>\n       and a grocer in\n       <a href="/wiki/Cheshire,_Connecticut" title="Cheshire, Connecticut">\n        Cheshire\n       </a>\n       for donations to assist the residents affected in\n       <a href="/wiki/Effects_of_Hurricane_Isabel_in_North_Carolina" title="Effects of Hurricane Isabel in North Carolina">\n        North Carolina\n       </a>\n       . A few days after Isabel moved ashore, the officials sent a tractor trailer truck with 2,000\xc2\xa0gallons (7570\xc2\xa0l) of water and 25,000\xc2\xa0pounds (11,340\xc2\xa0kg) of ice to North Carolina.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cema_17-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-cema-17">\n         [17]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       In Vermont, the strong pressure gradient produced a peak wind gust of 55\xc2\xa0mph (89\xc2\xa0km/h) in\n       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Pleasant_Valley,_Vermont&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Pleasant Valley, Vermont (page does not exist)">\n        Pleasant Valley\n       </a>\n       , causing sporadic downed trees and power lines throughout the state.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-verncdc1_18-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-verncdc1-18">\n         [18]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       In\n       <a href="/wiki/Richmond,_Vermont" title="Richmond, Vermont">\n        Richmond\n       </a>\n       , trees fell on and damaged three\xc2\xa0trucks,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-verncdc2_19-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-verncdc2-19">\n         [19]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and in\n       <a href="/wiki/Salisbury,_Vermont" title="Salisbury, Vermont">\n        Salisbury\n       </a>\n       a falling tree damaged a car.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-verncdc3_20-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-verncdc3-20">\n         [20]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Damage in the state totaled about $100,000 (2003\xc2\xa0USD, $117,000 in 2008\xc2\xa0USD).\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-verncdc1_18-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-verncdc1-18">\n         [18]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-verncdc2_19-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-verncdc2-19">\n         [19]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-verncdc3_20-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-verncdc3-20">\n         [20]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-verncdc4_21-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-verncdc4-21">\n         [21]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-verncdc5_22-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-verncdc5-22">\n         [22]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-verncdc6_23-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-verncdc6-23">\n         [23]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Rainfall reached 1\xc2\xa0inch (25\xc2\xa0mm) in various portions of\n       <a href="/wiki/New_Hampshire" title="New Hampshire">\n        New Hampshire\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/Maine" title="Maine">\n        Maine\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rain_11-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-rain-11">\n         [11]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n
