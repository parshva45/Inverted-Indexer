<p>\n       <b>\n        Paolo Angelo Nespoli\n       </b>\n       (\n       <a href="/wiki/Milan" title="Milan">\n        Milan\n       </a>\n       , 6 April 1957) is an Italian\n       <a href="/wiki/Astronaut" title="Astronaut">\n        astronaut\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/Engineer" title="Engineer">\n        engineer\n       </a>\n       of the European Space Agency (ESA). In 2007, he first traveled into space aboard the\n       <a href="/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Discovery" title="Space Shuttle Discovery">\n        Space Shuttle Discovery\n       </a>\n       as a mission specialist of\n       <a href="/wiki/STS-120" title="STS-120">\n        STS-120\n       </a>\n       . In December 2010 he again traveled into space aboard the\n       <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-20" title="Soyuz TMA-20">\n        Soyuz TMA-20\n       </a>\n       spacecraft as an\n       <a href="/wiki/Expedition_26" title="Expedition 26">\n        Expedition 26\n       </a>\n       /\n       <a href="/wiki/Expedition_27" title="Expedition 27">\n        27\n       </a>\n       <a href="/wiki/Flight_engineer" title="Flight engineer">\n        flight engineer\n       </a>\n       . Nespoli\'s latest mission is called VITA. He launched on Soyuz MS-05 on July 28, 2017 15:41 UTC and is part of Expedition 52/53.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n      </p>\n      <div class="toc" id="toc">\n       <div class="toctitle">\n </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       Nespoli has received the following awards:\n      </p>\n      <ul>\n       <li>\n        2007\n        <a class="mw-redirect"   2009 Cavaliere dell\'\n        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ordine_della_Stella_della_solidariet%C3%A0_italiana" title="Ordine della Stella della solidariet\xc3\xa0 italiana">\n         Ordine della Stella della solidariet\xc3\xa0 italiana\n        </a>\n       </li>\n       <li>\n        2015\n        <i>\n         America Award\n        </i>\n        of the\n        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Italy-USA_Foundation" title="Italy-USA Foundation">\n         Italy-USA Foundation\n        </a>\n       </li>\n      </ul>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline"       </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       In July 1998, he was selected as an astronaut for\n       <a href="/wiki/Italian_Space_Agency" title="Italian Space Agency">\n        Italian Space Agency\n       </a>\n       (ASI) and in August 1998, Nespoli was assigned by the\n       <a href="/wiki/European_Space_Agency" title="European Space Agency">\n        European Space Agency\n       </a>\n       to train at NASA\xe2\x80\x99s\n       <a href="/wiki/Johnson_Space_Center" title="Johnson Space Center">\n        Johnson Space Center\n       </a>\n       in\n       <a href="/wiki/Houston" title="Houston">\n        Houston\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Texas" title="Texas">\n        Texas\n       </a>\n       .\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="STS-120">\n        STS-120\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Paolo_Nespoli&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: STS-120">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <div class="thumb tleft">\n       <div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;">\n        <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:S120e006445.jpg">\n         <img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="2007" data-file-width="3032" height="146" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/4a/S120e006445.jpg/220px-S120e006445.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/4a/S120e006445.jpg/330px-S120e006445.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/4a/S120e006445.jpg/440px-S120e006445.jpg 2x" width="220"/>\n        </a>\n        <div class="thumbcaption">\n         <div class="magnify">\n          <a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:S120e006445.jpg" title="Enlarge">\n          </a>\n         </div>\n         <a href="/wiki/STS-120" title="STS-120">\n          STS-120\n         </a>\n         mission specialist Paolo Nespoli in the\n         <a href="/wiki/International_Space_Station" title="International Space Station">\n          International Space Station\n         </a>\n         .\n        </div>\n       </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       On 23 October 2007 Paolo launched on board\n       <a href="/wiki/STS-120" title="STS-120">\n        STS-120\n       </a>\n       to the\n       <a href="/wiki/International_Space_Station" title="International Space Station">\n        International Space Station\n       </a>\n       ; the\n       <a href="/wiki/Space_Shuttle" title="Space Shuttle">\n        Space Shuttle\n       </a>\n       mission which delivered the\n       <i>\n        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Harmony_Module" title="Harmony Module">\n         Harmony\n        </a>\n       </i>\n       module (formerly known as Node 2) to the International Space Station.\n       <i>\n        Harmony\n       </i>\n       was built by\n       <a href="/wiki/Thales_Alenia_Space" title="Thales Alenia Space">\n        Thales Alenia Space\n       </a>\n       at its facility in\n       <a href="/wiki/Turin" title="Turin">\n        Turin\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">\n        Italy\n       </a>\n       . He participated as a mission specialist and remained in space for 15 days, 2 hours and 23 minutes. During STS-120, he participated in the\n       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Esperia_mission&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Esperia mission (page does not exist)">\n        Esperia mission\n       </a>\n       for the\n       <a href="/wiki/European_Space_Agency" title="European Space Agency">\n        European Space Agency\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span id="Expedition_26/27_\'MagISStra\'">\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Expedition_26.2F27_.27MagISStra.27">\n        Expedition 26/27 \'MagISStra\'\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Paolo_Nespoli&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Expedition 26/27 \'MagISStra\'">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <div class="thumb tright">\n       <div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;">\n        <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:ISS-26_Paolo_Nespoli_with_Robonaut2.jpg">\n         <img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="2832" data-file-width="4256" height="146" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6d/ISS-26_Paolo_Nespoli_with_Robonaut2.jpg/220px-ISS-26_Paolo_Nespoli_with_Robonaut2.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6d/ISS-26_Paolo_Nespoli_with_Robonaut2.jpg/330px-ISS-26_Paolo_Nespoli_with_Robonaut2.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6d/ISS-26_Paolo_Nespoli_with_Robonaut2.jpg/440px-ISS-26_Paolo_Nespoli_with_Robonaut2.jpg 2x" width="220"/>\n        </a>\n        <div class="thumbcaption">\n         <div class="magnify">\n          <a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:ISS-26_Paolo_Nespoli_with_Robonaut2.jpg" title="Enlarge">\n          </a>\n         </div>\n         Expedition 26/27 flight engineer Paolo Nespoli poses with\n         <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Robonaut_2" title="Robonaut 2">\n          Robonaut 2\n         </a>\n         .\n        </div>\n       </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       Paolo Nespoli served as first flight engineer for\n       <a href="/wiki/Expedition_26" title="Expedition 26">\n        Expedition 26\n       </a>\n       /\n       <a href="/wiki/Expedition_27" title="Expedition 27">\n        27\n       </a>\n       , Europe\xe2\x80\x99s third six-month mission to the\n       <a href="/wiki/International_Space_Station" title="International Space Station">\n        International Space Station\n       </a>\n       (ISS).\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       On 15 December 2010 Nespoli flew aboard the\n       <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-20" title="Soyuz TMA-20">\n        Soyuz TMA-20\n       </a>\n       spacecraft from\n       <a href="/wiki/Baikonur_Cosmodrome" title="Baikonur Cosmodrome">\n        Baikonur Cosmodrome\n       </a>\n       in\n       <a href="/wiki/Kazakhstan" title="Kazakhstan">\n        Kazakhstan\n       </a>\n       to the International Space Station with the Russian cosmonaut\n       <a href="/wiki/Dmitri_Kondratyev" title="Dmitri Kondratyev">\n        Dmitri Kondratyev\n       </a>\n       and NASA\xe2\x80\x99s astronaut\n       <a href="/wiki/Catherine_Coleman" title="Catherine Coleman">\n        Catherine Coleman\n       </a>\n       . The three members of the crew returned to Earth in 24 May 2011. This mission, dubbed \xe2\x80\x98MagISStra\xe2\x80\x99, is Paolo Nespoli\xe2\x80\x99s second flight in space.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       From 15 December 2010 to 24 May 2011, Paolo Nespoli\xe2\x80\x99s duties\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MagISStra_4-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-MagISStra-4">\n         [4]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       aboard the ISS included participating in the docking operations to receive Europe\xe2\x80\x99s second\n       <a href="/wiki/Automated_Transfer_Vehicle" title="Automated Transfer Vehicle">\n        Automated Transfer Vehicle\n       </a>\n       (ATV-2) "Johannes Kepler", a visiting spacecraft that will deliver essential cargo to the Station. In early January, Nespoli filmed the majority of the footage for the documentary film\n       <i>\n        <a href="/wiki/First_Orbit" title="First Orbit">\n         First Orbit\n        </a>\n       </i>\n       , and as a result is credited as its\n       <a href="/wiki/Cinematographer" title="Cinematographer">\n        director of photography\n       </a>\n       .\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Nespoli took part in the arrival of the second Japanese HII Transfer Vehicle (\n       <a href="/wiki/H-II_Transfer_Vehicle" title="H-II Transfer Vehicle">\n        HTV-2\n       </a>\n       ), an unmanned spacecraft used to resupply the ISS. He was the prime operator for berthing the HTV-2 to the ISS after the free-flying vehicle was captured by NASA astronaut Catherine Coleman. In May 2011,\n       <a href="/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Endeavour" title="Space Shuttle Endeavour">\n        Space Shuttle Endeavour\n       </a>\n       has delivered the\n       <a href="/wiki/Alpha_Magnetic_Spectrometer" title="Alpha Magnetic Spectrometer">\n        Alpha Magnetic Spectrometer\n       </a>\n       (AMS-02) to the ISS.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       During Nespoli\'s stint with Expedition 27, his mother, Maria, died on 4 May 2011. The crew observed 1 minute of silence the following day around the time of her funeral.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5">\n        <a href="#cite_note-5">\n         [5]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Paolo Nespoli carried out an intensive programme of experiments in the Station, ranging from radiation monitoring to measurements that could improve oil recovery in petroleum reservoirs. The mission scientific programme covered different fields on human research, fluid physics, radiation, biology and technology demonstrations.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Nespoli contributed to the scientific exploitation of Europe\xe2\x80\x99s Columbus laboratory. As an astronaut, he carried out several experiments for ESA, NASA and also the Japanese and Canadian space agencies. During the mission, Paolo participated in some educational activities: the educational programme\n       <a class="external text" href="http://trainlikeanastronaut.org/" rel="nofollow">\n        "Mission X: Train Like an Astronaut"\n       </a>\n       which gave children the chance to follow an international initiative built around health, well-being and nutrition. He also participated in a greenhouse activity in space. Nespoli used ESA\xe2\x80\x99s novel 3D camera to show images of the ISS.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       As Paolo left the ISS on 23 May 2011 in the\n       <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-20" title="Soyuz TMA-20">\n        Soyuz TMA-20\n       </a>\n       he was able to take the first pictures of a space shuttle docked with the ISS from the perspective of a Russian\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Soyuz_spacecraft" title="Soyuz spacecraft">\n        Soyuz spacecraft\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6">\n        <a href="#cite_note-6">\n         [6]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span id="Expedition_52/53_and_the_VITA_mission">\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Expedition_52.2F53_and_the_VITA_mission">\n        Expedition 52/53 and the VITA mission\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Paolo_Nespoli&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Expedition 52/53 and the VITA mission">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <div class="thumb tright">\n       <div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;">\n        <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:VITA_mission_logo.jpg">\n         <img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="316" data-file-width="316" height="220" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/0/0c/VITA_mission_logo.jpg/220px-VITA_mission_logo.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/0/0c/VITA_mission_logo.jpg 1.5x" width="220"/>\n        </a>\n        <div class="thumbcaption">\n         <div class="magnify">\n          <a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:VITA_mission_logo.jpg" title="Enlarge">\n          </a>\n         </div>\n         VITA mission logo\n        </div>\n       </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       Nespoli is part of Expedition 52/53, which started in 2017. He launched on\n       <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_MS-05" title="Soyuz MS-05">\n        Soyuz MS-05\n       </a>\n       on July 28, 2017 15:41 UTC\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sfi-20170728_7-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-sfi-20170728-7">\n         [7]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       . Nespoli\'s mission to the ISS is called\n       <b>\n        VITA\n       </b>\n       . Vita is an acronym for Vitality, Innovation, Technology and Ability. In addition, in Italian it means life, reflecting the scientific experiments and the technologies needed for life in space. Additional activities will include outreach like Mission-X: Train Like an Astronaut, the European Astro Pi Challenge (where European students run their own code on Raspberry Pi mini computers installed on the ISS).\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-VITA_8-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-VITA-8">\n         [8]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h4>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="VITA_Mission_Logo">\n        VITA Mission Logo\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Paolo_Nespoli&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: VITA Mission Logo">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h4>\n      <p>\n       The mission\'s logo was developed jointly by\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ESA" title="ESA">\n        ESA\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Italian_Space_Agency" title="Italian Space Agency">\n        Italian Space Agency\n       </a>\n       , and Nespoli. The overall circle represent the planet Earth, with the main objectives linked by a symbol which is a reformulation of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Infinity" title="Infinity">\n        infinity\n       </a>\n       symbol, called "Third paradise" and designed by Michelangelo Pistoletto. Connected inside are a\n       <a href="/wiki/DNA" title="DNA">\n        DNA\n       </a>\n       strand for the scientific experiments, a book for education, outreach and culture, and Earth as a symbol of humanity. The colors represent the Italian flag.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Vita_logo_9-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-Vita_logo-9">\n         [9]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n
