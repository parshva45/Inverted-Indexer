   </table>\n      <p>\n       The\n       <b>\n        2017\xe2\x80\x9318 Australian region cyclone season\n       </b>\n       is the period of the year when most\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclones" title="Tropical cyclones">\n        tropical cyclones\n       </a>\n       form in the Southern\n       <a href="/wiki/Indian_Ocean" title="Indian Ocean">\n        Indian Ocean\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">\n        Pacific Oceans\n       </a>\n       between 90\xc2\xb0E and 160\xc2\xb0E. The season officially began 1 November 2017 and will end 30 April 2018; however, tropical cyclones can form at any time of the year, as demonstrated by the first tropical low of the season in early August. Any tropical system that forms between 1 July 2017 and 30 June 2018 will count towards the season total. During the season, tropical cyclones will be officially monitored by one of the five tropical cyclone warning centres (TCWCs) that operate in this region. Three of the five centres are operated by the Australian\n       <a href="/wiki/Bureau_of_Meteorology" title="Bureau of Meteorology">\n        Bureau of Meteorology\n       </a>\n       (BOM) in\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Perth,_Western_Australia" title="Perth, Western Australia">\n        Perth\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Darwin,_Northern_Territory" title="Darwin, Northern Territory">\n        Darwin\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Brisbane,_Queensland" title="Brisbane, Queensland">\n        Brisbane\n       </a>\n       , while the other two are operated by the National Weather Service of\n       <a href="/wiki/Papua_New_Guinea" title="Papua New Guinea">\n        Papua New Guinea\n       </a>\n       in\n       <a href="/wiki/Port_Moresby" title="Port Moresby">\n        Port Moresby\n       </a>\n       and the\n       <a href="/wiki/Indonesian_Agency_for_Meteorology,_Climatology_and_Geophysics" title="Indonesian Agency for Meteorology, Climatology and Geophysics">\n        Indonesian Agency for Meteorology, Climatology and Geophysics\n       </a>\n       in\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jakarta,_Indonesia" title="Jakarta, Indonesia">\n        Jakarta\n       </a>\n       . The\n       <a href="/wiki/Joint_Typhoon_Warning_Center" title="Joint Typhoon Warning Center">\n        Joint Typhoon Warning Center\n       </a>\n       (JTWC) of the\n       <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">\n        United States\n       </a>\n       and other national meteorological services including\n       <a href="/wiki/M%C3%A9t%C3%A9o-France"
