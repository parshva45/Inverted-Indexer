<i>\n        <b>\n         Destiny\n        </b>\n       </i>\n       module, also known as the US Lab, is the primary operating facility for\n       <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United construction of the 16 ton (14.5 tonne), state-of-the art research laboratory in 1995 at the\n       <a href="/wiki/Marshall_Space_Flight_Center" title="Marshall Space Flight Center">\n        Marshall Space Flight Center\n       </a>\n       in\n       <a href="/wiki/Huntsville,_Alabama" title="Huntsville, Alabama">\n        Huntsville\n       </a>\n       , Alabama.\n       <sup commercial applications.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lab_3-7">\n        <a href="#cite_note-lab-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The laboratory has a total of 24 href="/wiki/International_Standard_Payload_Rack" title="International Standard Payload Rack">\n        International Standard Payload Racks\n       </a>\n       (ISPRs), that can be removed or <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-spaceandtech_9-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-spaceandtech-9">\n         [9]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The eight rack bays are equipped with curtains
