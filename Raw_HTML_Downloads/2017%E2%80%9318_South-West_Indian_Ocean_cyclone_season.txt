</a>\n       </sup>\n       moved west-southwest across the\n       <a href="/wiki/90th_meridian_east" title="90th meridian east">\n        90th meridian east\n       </a>\n       into the Southwest  </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <div style="clear:both;">\n      </div>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Storm_names">\n        Storm names\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=2017%E2%80%9318_South-West_Indian_Ocean_cyclone_season&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Storm names">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       Within the South-West Indian Ocean, tropical depressions and subtropical depressions that are judged to have 10-minute sustained wind speeds of 65\xc2\xa0km/h (40\xc2\xa0mph) by the\n       <a href="/wiki/Regional_Specialized_Meteorological_Center" title="Regional Specialized Meteorological Center">\n        Regional Specialized Meteorological Center\n       </a>\n       on\n       <a href="/wiki/R%C3%A9union" title="R\xc3\xa9union">\n        La R\xc3\xa9union Island\n       </a>\n       , France (RSMC La R\xc3\xa9union) are usually assigned a name. However, it is the Sub-Regional Tropical Cyclone Advisory Centers in Mauritius and Madagascar who name the systems. The Sub-Regional Tropical Cyclone Advisory Center in Mauritius names a storm should it intensify into a moderate tropical storm between\n       <a href="/wiki/55th_meridian_east" title="55th meridian east">\n        55\xc2\xb0E\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/90th_meridian_east" title="90th meridian east">\n        90\xc2\xb0E\n       </a>\n       . If instead a cyclone intensifies into a moderate tropical storm between\n       <a href="/wiki/30th_meridian_east" title="30th meridian east">\n        30\xc2\xb0E\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/55th_meridian_east" title="55th meridian east">\n        55\xc2\xb0E\n       </a>\n       then the Sub-Regional Tropical Cyclone Advisory Center in\n       <a href="/wiki/Madagascar" title="Madagascar">\n        Madagascar\n       </a>\n       assigns the appropriate name to the storm. Beginning from the 2016\xe2\x80\x9317 season, name lists within the South-West Indian Ocean will be rotated on a triennial basis. Storm names are only used once, so any storm name used this year will be removed from rotation and replaced with a new name for the 2020\xe2\x80\x9321 season. The unused names are expected to be reused in the list for the 2020\xe2\x80\x9321 season.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TCOP-SWIO_4-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-TCOP-SWIO-4">\n
