After initial training, cosmonauts are assigned as either a test-cosmonaut (\xd0\xba\xd0\xbe\xd1\x81\xd0\xbc\xd0\xbe\xd0\xbd\xd0\xb0\xd0\xb2\xd1\x82-\xd0\xb8\xd1\x81\xd0\xbf\xd1\x8b\xd1\x82\xd0\xb0\xd1\x82\xd0\xb5\xd0\xbb\xd1\x8c, kosmonavt-ispytatel\xe2\x80\x99) or a research-cosmonaut (\xd0\xba\xd0\xbe\xd1\x81\xd0\xbc\xd0\xbe\xd0\xbd\xd0\xb0\xd0\xb2\xd1\x82-\xd0\xb8\xd1\x81\xd1\x81\xd0\xbb\xd0\xb5\xd0\xb4\xd0\xbe\xd0\xb2\xd0\xb0\xd1\x82\xd0\xb5\xd0\xbb\xd1\x8c, kosmonavt-issledovatel\xe2\x80\x99). class="reference" id="cite_ref-6">\n        <a href="#cite_note-6">\n         [6]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Higher ranks include pilot-cosmonaut, test-cosmonaut
