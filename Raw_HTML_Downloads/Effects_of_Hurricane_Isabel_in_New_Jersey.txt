Isabel in New Jersey\n       </b>\n       in 2003 were overall moderate, limited to fallen trees, two deaths, and $50\xc2\xa0million in damage (2003\xc2\xa0USD, $59\xc2\xa0million 2008\xc2\xa0USD).\n       <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Isabel" title="Hurricane Isabel">\n        Hurricane Isabel\n       </a>\n       formed from a\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_wave" title="Tropical wave">\n        tropical wave\n       </a>\n       on September 6 in the tropical\n       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">\n        Atlantic Ocean\n       </a>\n       . It moved northwestward, and within an environment of light wind shear and warm waters it steadily strengthened to reach peak winds of 165\xc2\xa0mph (265\xc2\xa0km/h) on September 11. After fluctuating in intensity for four days, Isabel gradually weakened and made landfall on the\n       <a href="/wiki/Outer_Banks" title="Outer Banks">\n        Outer Banks\n       </a>\n       of\n       <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina" title="North Carolina">\n        North Carolina\n       </a>\n       with winds of 105\xc2\xa0mph (165\xc2\xa0km/h) on September 18. It quickly weakened over land and became extratropical over western\n       <a href="/wiki/Pennsylvania" title="Pennsylvania">\n        Pennsylvania\n       </a>\n       the next day. Several days before Isabel made landfall, there existed uncertainty in where the hurricane would strike. At least one computer model predicted a landfall on\n       <a href="/wiki/New_Jersey" title="New Jersey">\n        New Jersey\n       </a>\n       , and as a result services across the state thoroughly prepared for the hurricane.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tcr_1-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-tcr-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Isabel passed 215\xc2\xa0miles (350\xc2\xa0km) southwest of the state, though its large wind core produced tropical storm force winds across much of the state. The winds downed hundreds of trees and power lines, leaving hundreds of thousands without power. A falling tree killed one person. Hurricane Isabel produced rough waves and a moderate storm surge along the coastline. One person was killed from the rough waves, and at least 50 locations along the\n       <a href="/wiki/Jersey_Shore" title="Jersey Shore">\n        Jersey Shore\n       </a>\n       reported beach erosion from the hurricane.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n      </p>\n      <div class="toc" id="toc">\n       <div class="toctitle">\n        <h2>\n         Contents\n        </h2>\n       </div>\n   Hurricane Isabel to the southeast of North Carolina\n        </div>\n       </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       44\xc2\xa0hours before Hurricane Isabel made landfall on the Outer Banks of North   The National Hurricane Center also briefly issued a hurricane watch for the New Jersey coastline.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-assessment_2-0">\n        <a   </a>\n       </sup>\n       By four days before the hurricane struck land, at least one\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Global_climate_model" title="Global climate model">\n        computer model\n       </a>\n       predicted Isabel would strike New Jersey.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-avila33_4-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-avila33-4">\n         [4]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       News stations were stationed with crews along the Jersey shore several days in advance of Isabel to provide breaking news and live conditions.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-poynter_5-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-poynter-5">\n         [5]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Many residents prepared their houses by boarding windows and purchasing emergency supplies. The\n       <a href="/wiki/Sussex_County,_New_Jersey" title="Sussex County, New Jersey">\n        Sussex County\n       </a>\n       chapter of the\n       <a href="/wiki/American_Red_Cross" title="American Red Cross">\n        American Red Cross\n       </a>\n       advised local high schools to be on stand-by as potential shelters in the event evacuation occurred.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-patmindos_6-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-patmindos-6">\n         [6]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Emergency coordinators in several counties were on alert, though none issued evacuations.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-arrlweb_7-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-arrlweb-7">\n         [7]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       In preparation for anticipated power outages, the\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jersey_Central_Power_and_Light" title="Jersey Central Power and Light">\n        Jersey Central Power and Light\n       </a>\n       company arranged to receive more electrical crews from its parent company,\n       <a href="/wiki/FirstEnergy" title="FirstEnergy">\n        FirstEnergy\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-patmindos_6-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-patmindos-6">\n         [6]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Other utility workers from various locations as far as\n       <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">\n        Canada\n       </a>\n       left for the state in the event of power outages.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-foxnews_8-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-foxnews-8">\n         [8]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Several flights in and out of the state were delayed or canceled,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cnn918_9-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-cnn918-9">\n         [9]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and the\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cape_May-Lewes_Ferry" title="Cape May-Lewes Ferry">\n        Cape May-Lewes Ferry\n       </a>\n       canceled travel across the\n       <a href="/wiki/Delaware_Bay" title="Delaware Bay">\n        Delaware Bay\n       </a>\n       during the duration of Isabel. In Atlantic City, casino workers prepared for coastal flooding by placing sandbags at boardwalk entrances.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-foxnews_8-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-foxnews-8">\n         [8]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/New_Jersey_Transit" title="New Jersey Transit">\n        New Jersey Transit\n       </a>\n       workers secured its buses, railways, and light rail equipment. To ensure service would remain accessible during and after the hurricane, NJ Transit prepared backup generators, pumps, and chainsaws, with workers inspecting trains and the paths of the lines.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-njtransit_10-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-njtransit-10">\n         [10]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       FEMA mobilized and dispatched an\n       <a href="/wiki/FEMA_Urban_Search_and_Rescue_Task_Force" title="FEMA Urban Search and Rescue Task Force">\n        Urban Search and Rescue Task Force\n       </a>\n       of 28\xc2\xa0people to the state for possible rescue duty.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-dhs_11-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-dhs-11">\n         [11]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Days before the storm made landfall, the\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Salvation_Army" title="Salvation Army">\n        Salvation Army\n       </a>\n       prepared food and aid for potentially affected citizens.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-salavation_12-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-salavation-12">\n         [12]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       In anticipation for the effects of Isabel,\n       <a href="/wiki/Governor_of_New_Jersey" title="Governor of New Jersey">\n        Governor\n       </a>\n       <a href="/wiki/Jim_McGreevey" title="Jim McGreevey">\n        Jim McGreevey\n       </a>\n       declared a\n       <a href="/wiki/State_of_emergency#United_States" title="State of emergency">\n        state of emergency\n       </a>\n       , which allowed the\n       <a href="/wiki/Federal_Emergency_Management_Agency" title="Federal Emergency Management Agency">\n        Federal Emergency Management Agency\n       </a>\n       to deal with the situation.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mikedererap918_13-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-mikedererap918-13">\n         [13]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Impact">\n        Impact\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Effects_of_Hurricane_Isabel_in_New_Jersey&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Impact">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       Isabel produced a\n       <a href="/wiki/Storm_surge" title="Storm surge">\n        storm surge\n       </a>\n       along the New Jersey coastline of up to 6.5\xc2\xa0feet (2\xc2\xa0m) in\n       <a href="/wiki/Cape_May,_New_Jersey" title="Cape May, New Jersey">\n        Cape May\n       </a>\n       . Higher amounts occurred along the\n       <a href="/wiki/Delaware_River" title="Delaware River">\n        Delaware River\n       </a>\n       , peaking in the state at 10.6\xc2\xa0feet (3.2\xc2\xa0m) in\n       <a href="/wiki/Burlington,_New_Jersey" title="Burlington, New Jersey">\n        Burlington\n       </a>\n       . At its closest approach, Isabel passed within about 215\xc2\xa0miles (350\xc2\xa0km) of the state, and as a result the outer\n       <a href="/wiki/Rainband" title="Rainband">\n        rainbands\n       </a>\n       produced light rainfall.\n       <a href="/wiki/Wildwood,_New_Jersey" title="Wildwood, New Jersey">\n        Wildwood\n       </a>\n       along the southeast coast reported 1.3\xc2\xa0inches (33\xc2\xa0mm) of rain,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tcr_1-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-tcr-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       while\n       <a href="/wiki/Lincoln_Park,_New_Jersey" title="Lincoln Park, New Jersey">\n        Lincoln Park\n       </a>\n       in the northern portion of the state recorded 2.07\xc2\xa0inches (52\xc2\xa0mm) of precipitation.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-njnws_14-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-njnws-14">\n         [14]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The large wind field of the hurricane produced moderate winds across the state. A\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bar_(landform)" title="Bar (landform)">\n        shoal\n       </a>\n       in the Delaware Bay experienced sustained winds of 54\xc2\xa0mph (87\xc2\xa0km/h) with gusts to 71\xc2\xa0mph (114\xc2\xa0km/h). Cape May reported tropical storm force winds with gusts to 61\xc2\xa0mph (98\xc2\xa0km/h), while\n       <a href="/wiki/Newark,_New_Jersey" title="Newark, New Jersey">\n        Newark\n       </a>\n       in the northern portion of the state experienced gusts to 44\xc2\xa0mph (71\xc2\xa0km/h).\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tcr_1-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-tcr-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Hurricane Isabel produced slightly above normal tides and rough surf along the Jersey shore, killing one surfer off of\n       <a href="/wiki/Wildwood_Crest,_New_Jersey" title="Wildwood Crest, New Jersey">\n        Wildwood Crest\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-njncdc_15-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-njncdc-15">\n         [15]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The combination of gusty winds and the heavy surf produced moderate beach erosion along much of the coastline, primarily to beaches facing southeastward. In the\n       <a href="/wiki/Delaware_Bay" title="Delaware Bay">\n        Delaware Bay\n       </a>\n       and River area, no significant erosion was reported, though coastal flooding from the hurricane washed out a road and destroyed the deck of a house in\n       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Baypoint,_New_Jersey&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Baypoint, New Jersey (page does not exist)">\n        Baypoint\n       </a>\n       . In\n       <a href="/wiki/Cape_May_County,_New_Jersey" title="Cape May County, New Jersey">\n        Cape May County\n       </a>\n       waves eroded the beaches by up to 4\xc2\xa0feet (1.2\xc2\xa0m) in\n       <a href="/wiki/Ocean_City,_New_Jersey" title="Ocean City, New Jersey">\n        Ocean City\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/Avalon,_New_Jersey" title="Avalon, New Jersey">\n        Avalon\n       </a>\n       , with several cities experiencing a loss of dunes and\n       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Geotube&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Geotube (page does not exist)">\n        geotubes\n       </a>\n       in\n       <a href="/wiki/Strathmere,_New_Jersey" title="Strathmere, New Jersey">\n        Whale Beach\n       </a>\n       being exposed. Waves from Isabel in\n       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_County,_New_Jersey" title="Atlantic County, New Jersey">\n        Atlantic County\n       </a>\n       resulted in light damage, minor coastal flooding, and slight beach erosion. The beaches of southern\n       <a href="/wiki/Ocean_County,_New_Jersey" title="Ocean County, New Jersey">\n        Ocean County\n       </a>\n       experienced a loss of 3 to 4\xc2\xa0feet (0.9 to 1.2\xc2\xa0m) of sand, while locations further to the north experienced only minor erosion. Most coastal areas of\n       <a href="/wiki/Monmouth_County,_New_Jersey" title="Monmouth County, New Jersey">\n        Monmouth County\n       </a>\n       reported eroded beaches by up to 4\xc2\xa0feet (1.2\xc2\xa0m), with\n       <a href="/wiki/Union_Beach,_New_Jersey" title="Union Beach, New Jersey">\n        Union Beach\n       </a>\n       losing about 5,000\xc2\xa0sq. feet (465\xc2\xa0sq. m) of sand.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-beachy_16-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-beachy-16">\n         [16]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Moderate wind gusts throughout the state downed hundreds of trees, tree limbs, and power lines. Over 382,000\xc2\xa0people were without power, one of the worst power outages on record for area utilities.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-njncdc_15-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-njncdc-15">\n         [15]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Downed trees and power lines closed major streets and schools in\n       <a href="/wiki/Union_County,_New_Jersey" title="Union County, New Jersey">\n        Union County\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-njncdc2_17-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-njncdc2-17">\n         [17]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       One downed tree greatly damaged a house in\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Middletown,_New_Jersey" title="Middletown, New Jersey">\n        Middletown\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-scotsman_18-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-scotsman-18">\n         [18]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and in\n       <a href="/wiki/Hudson_County,_New_Jersey" title="Hudson County, New Jersey">\n        Hudson County\n       </a>\n       several trees fell onto and damaged cars. A downed tree in\n       <a href="/wiki/Englewood,_New_Jersey" title="Englewood, New Jersey">\n        Englewood\n       </a>\n       injured a woman when she was struck,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-njncdc2_17-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-njncdc2-17">\n         [17]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and a woman in\n       <a href="/wiki/Independence_Township,_New_Jersey" title="Independence Township, New Jersey">\n        Independence Township\n       </a>\n       was killed when a fallen tree landed on the vehicle she was driving.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-njncdc_15-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-njncdc-15">\n         [15]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Strong winds from Isabel blew out the windows in an office building in\n       <a href="/wiki/East_Rutherford,_New_Jersey" title="East Rutherford, New Jersey">\n        East Rutherford\n       </a>\n       , causing injuries to two women when they were struck.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-njncdc_15-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-njncdc-15">\n         [15]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Throughout the state, damage totaled to about $50\xc2\xa0million (2003\xc2\xa0USD, $59\xc2\xa0million 2008\xc2\xa0USD).\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tcr_1-4">\n        <a href="#cite_note-tcr-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="See_also">\n        See also\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span
