id="cite_ref-russianspaceweb_1-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-russianspaceweb-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Poisk is overall the same design as a docking module Pirs.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Whereas Pirs has been attached to the\n       <i>\n        nadir\n       </i>\n       ("bottom") port of\n       <a href="/wiki/Zvezda_(ISS_module)" title="Zvezda (ISS module)">\n        <i>\n         Zvezda\n        </i>\n        module\n       </a>\n       , Poisk is Poisk\n       </i>\n       is Russian for\n       <i>\n        explore\n       </i>\n       or\n       <i>\n        search\n       </i>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">\n        <a href="#cite_note-4">\n         [4]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n has two egress hatches for EVA\'s in addition to the two spacecraft docking ports.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6">\n        <a href="#cite_note-6">\n         [6]\n        </a>\n
