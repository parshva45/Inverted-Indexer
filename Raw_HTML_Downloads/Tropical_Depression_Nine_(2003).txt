  <p>\n       <b>\n        Tropical Depression Nine\n       </b>\n       was a weak tropical depression that developed and dissipated in the eastern\n       <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Sea" title="Caribbean Sea">\n        Caribbean Sea\n       </a>\n       in August 2003. It formed from a\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_wave" title="Tropical wave">\n        tropical wave\n       </a>\n       on August 21 to the south of\n       <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">\n        Puerto Rico\n       </a>\n       , and was initially forecast to strengthen to tropical storm status, due to the\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclogenesis" title="Tropical cyclogenesis">\n        favorable conditions for development\n       </a>\n       in the area. However,\n       <a href="/wiki/Wind_shear" title="Wind shear">\n        wind shear\n       </a>\n       developed over the system, and the depression degenerated to a tropical wave on August 22. The system produced moderate to heavy rainfall throughout its path. In Puerto Rico, the rains flooded ten\xc2\xa0houses, while in the\n       <a href="/wiki/Dominican_Republic" title="Dominican Republic">\n        Dominican Republic\n       </a>\n       the precipitation led to overflown rivers and two injuries.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n      </p>\n      <div class="toc" id="toc">\n       <div class="toctitle">\n        <h2>\n         Contents\n <a href="/wiki/Saffir%E2%80%93Simpson_scale" title="Saffir\xe2\x80\x93Simpson scale">\n          Saffir\xe2\x80\x93Simpson scale\n         </a>\n        </div>\n       </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       A strong\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_wave" title="Tropical wave">\n        tropical wave\n       </a>\n       exited the coast of\n       <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">\n        Africa\n       </a>\n       on August 14,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tcr_1-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-tcr-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       moved westward as a disorganized system, and passed through the\n       <a href="/wiki/Cape_Verde" title="Cape Verde">\n        Cape Verde\n       </a>\n       islands on August 15.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug15two_2-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug15two-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       On August 17, a weak\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Low_pressure_area" title="Low pressure area">\n        area of low pressure\n       </a>\n       developed in the system about 750\xc2\xa0miles (1,200\xc2\xa0km) west-southwest of the Cape Verde islands.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug17two_3-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug17two-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Convection increased over the western portion of the wave axis,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug18two_4-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug18two-4">\n         [4]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and the system began to become better organized on August 18 while located about 1,250\xc2\xa0miles (2,000\xc2\xa0km) east of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Lesser_Antilles" title="Lesser Antilles">\n        Lesser Antilles\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug18two2_5-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug18two2-5">\n         [5]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       However, convection around the system diminished on August 19,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug19two_6-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug19two-6">\n         [6]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and later that day it began to move through the Lesser Antilles. Despite favorable upper-level winds and falling\n       <a href="/wiki/Atmospheric_pressure" title="Atmospheric pressure">\n        atmospheric pressures\n       </a>\n       , there were initially no signs of a circulation at the surface.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug19two2_7-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug19two2-7">\n         [7]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Convection again increased early on August 20,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug19two3_8-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug19two3-8">\n         [8]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and it gradually became better organized.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug20two_9-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug20two-9">\n         [9]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Based on a ship\'s wind report of the system, it is estimated the wave developed into Tropical Depression Nine on August 21 while located about 260\xc2\xa0miles (415\xc2\xa0km) south of\n       <a href="/wiki/San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Juan, Puerto Rico">\n        San Juan\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">\n        Puerto Rico\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-td9disc1_10-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-td9disc1-10">\n         [10]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       The depression quickly showed signs of organization with the development of\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rainbands" title="Rainbands">\n        banding features\n       </a>\n       around its blossoming convection. Due to a favorable upper level environment, forecasters predicted the depression to intensify to a 70\xc2\xa0mph (110\xc2\xa0km/h) tropical storm after making landfall on\n       <a href="/wiki/Haiti" title="Haiti">\n        Haiti\n       </a>\n       and eastern\n       <a href="/wiki/Cuba" title="Cuba">\n        Cuba\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-td9disc1_10-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-td9disc1-10">\n         [10]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Upper-level outflow remained well-defined, though convection waned near the center early on August 22.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-td9disc2_11-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-td9disc2-11">\n         [11]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The depression appeared to continue organizing, with a well-defined cloud band to its north and east.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-td9disc4_12-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-td9disc4-12">\n         [12]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       However, strong southwesterly\n       <a href="/wiki/Wind_shear" title="Wind shear">\n        wind shear\n       </a>\n       unexpectedly increased over the system, and the depression degenerated into a tropical wave late on August 22, south of the eastern tip of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Dominican_Republic" title="Dominican Republic">\n        Dominican Republic\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tcr_1-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-tcr-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The system retained a well-defined cloud pattern with a vigorous mid-level circulation, and forecasters at the\n       <a href="/wiki/National_Hurricane_Center" title="National Hurricane Center">\n        National Hurricane Center\n       </a>\n       indicated regeneration could have occurred at any time.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-td9disc5_13-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-td9disc5-13">\n         [13]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The wave axis moved over the Dominican Republic early on August 23,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug22two_14-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug22two-14">\n         [14]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and became disorganized over the mountainous terrain of\n       <a href="/wiki/Hispaniola" title="Hispaniola">\n        Hispaniola\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug23two_15-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug23two-15">\n         [15]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       There existed the potential for redevelopment as it moved into a potentially more favorable area,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug24two_16-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug24two-16">\n         [16]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       though the National Hurricane Center ceased monitoring the system on August 25 when redevelopment appeared unlikely.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aug25two_17-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-aug25two-17">\n         [17]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Preparations">\n        Preparations\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       Coinciding with the issuance of the first advisory on the tropical depression, the government of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Dominican_Republic" title="Dominican Republic">\n        Dominican Republic\n       </a>\n       issued a\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclone_warnings_and_watches#Tropical_Storm_Watch" title="Tropical cyclone warnings and watches">\n        tropical storm watch\n       </a>\n       from\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Barahona_(city)" title="Barahona (city)">\n        Barahona\n       </a>\n       to its border with\n       <a href="/wiki/Haiti" title="Haiti">\n        Haiti\n       </a>\n       , while a tropical storm watch was issued from Haiti\'s border with the Dominican Republic westward to\n       <a href="/wiki/Port-au-Prince" title="Port-au-Prince">\n        Port-au-Prince\n       </a>\n       . The\n       <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">\n        United States\n       </a>\n       \' National Hurricane Center in\n       <a href="/wiki/Miami" title="Miami">\n        Miami\n       </a>\n       recommended interests in eastern\n       <a href="/wiki/Cuba" title="Cuba">\n        Cuba\n       </a>\n       to monitor the progress of the storm.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-td9pd1_18-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-td9pd1-18">\n         [18]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       When the depression was 205\xc2\xa0miles (330\xc2\xa0km) south-southeast of\n       <a href="/wiki/Santo_Domingo" title="Santo Domingo">\n        Santo Domingo\n       </a>\n       , the Dominican Republic issued a\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclone_warnings_and_watches#Tropical_Storm_Warning" title="Tropical cyclone warnings and watches">\n        tropical storm warning\n       </a>\n       from\n       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Punta_Palenque&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Punta Palenque (page does not exist)">\n        Punta Palenque\n       </a>\n       to its border with Haiti.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-td9pd3_19-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-td9pd3-19">\n         [19]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       While located 155\xc2\xa0miles (250\xc2\xa0km) from the coast of\n       <a href="/wiki/Hispaniola" title="Hispaniola">\n        Hispaniola\n       </a>\n       , the government of the Dominican Republic extended the tropical storm warning eastward to\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Isla_Saona" title="Isla Saona">\n        Isla Saona\n       </a>\n       , while the government of Haiti upgraded its watch area to a tropical storm warning along the coast. The National Hurricane Center also recommended citizens in the\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bahamas" title="Bahamas">\n        Bahamas\n       </a>\n       to monitor the progress of the storm.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-td9pd4_20-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-td9pd4-20">\n         [20]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       After the heavy rainfall began in the Dominican Republic, officials evacuated citizens of low-lying areas.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-domrep_21-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-domrep-21">\n         [21]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Impact">\n        Impact\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Tropical_Depression_Nine_(2003)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Impact">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span  .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-st._lucia_22-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-st._lucia-22">\n         [22]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The depression caused moderate rainfall in\n       <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">\n        Puerto Rico\n       </a>\n       , where two to three inches (50 to 75\xc2\xa0mm) of\n       <a class="mw-redirect"   <a href="#cite_note-ncdc2-23">\n         [23]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The flooding from the rainfall entered ten\xc2\xa0houses and left some streets impassable.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ncdcpr_24-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-ncdcpr-24">\n         [24]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       A mudslide was reported in the eastern portion of the island.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ncdcland_25-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-ncdcland-25">\n         [25]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       A river in northeastern Puerto Rico surpassed its banks from flooding, though it returned to normal levels within hours.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ncdcriver_26-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-ncdcriver-26">\n         [26]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Damage in Puerto Rico totaled $20,000 (2003\xc2\xa0USD, $26\xc2\xa0thousand 2017\xc2\xa0USD).\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ncdcpr_24-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-ncdcpr-24">\n         [24]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       The depression dropped over 1\xc2\xa0inch (25\xc2\xa0mm) of precipitation across much of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Dominican_Republic" title="Dominican Republic">\n        Dominican Republic\n       </a>\n       ;\n       <a href="/wiki/Santo_Domingo" title="Santo Domingo">\n        Santo Domingo\n       </a>\n       reported a peak total of 3.9\xc2\xa0in (98\xc2\xa0mm). The rainfall led to flooding, primarily east and west of the capital city, while five rivers overflowed their banks. Several roads were inundated, obstructing traffic. The flooding collapsed a sports center and a house, injuring two people inside. Further inland, 160\xc2\xa0people had to be evacuated when more than 100\xc2\xa0houses were flooded. Crop damage was also reported. In\n       <a href="/wiki/Pedernales,_Dominican_Republic" title="Pedernales, Dominican Republic">\n        Pedernales\n       </a>\n       , gusty winds uprooted trees, several of which fell on power lines which caused power outages in the town.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wmo_27-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-wmo-27">\n         [27]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The rainfall was welcomed in the country, as conditions were dry in the preceding months.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-domrep_21-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-domrep-21">\n         [21]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Light flooding also occurred in eastern\n       <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">\n        Jamaica\n       </a>\n       , though damage there, if any,
