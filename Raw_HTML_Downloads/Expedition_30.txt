      </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a   ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <p>\n       Expedition 30 began with the departure of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Expedition_28" title="Expedition 28">\n        Expedition 28\n       </a>\n       /\n       <a href="/wiki/Expedition_29" title="Expedition 29">\n        29\n       </a>\n       crew on board the\n       <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-02M" title="Soyuz TMA-02M">\n        Soyuz TMA-02M\n       </a>\n       spacecraft, on 21 November 2011 at 23:00\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UTC" title="UTC">\n        UTC\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ex28_1-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-ex28-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       This left the Expedition 29/30 crew, who had docked with the ISS in the\n       <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-22" title="Soyuz TMA-22">\n        Soyuz TMA-22\n       </a>\n       spacecraft on 16 November 2011 at 05:24 UTC,\n       <sup class="reference" Soyuz TMA-22 launched on 14 November 2011 at 04:14 UTC, from\n       <a href="/wiki/Baikonur_Cosmodrome" title="Baikonur Cosmodrome">\n        Baikonur Cosmodrome\n       </a>\n       in\n       <a <span class="mw-headline" id="Soyuz_TMA-03M_arrival">\n        Soyuz TMA-03M arrival\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Expedition_30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Soyuz TMA-03M arrival">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <p>\n       The ISS was crewed by the first three Expedition 30 crewmembers for approximately five-and-a-half weeks. They were joined on 23 December 2011 by the Expedition 30/\n       <a href="/wiki/Expedition_31" title="Expedition 31">\n        31\n       </a>\n       crew,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-docking_8-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-docking-8">\n         [8]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       who were carried to the ISS by the\n       <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-03M" title="Soyuz TMA-03M">\n        Soyuz TMA-03M\n       </a>\n       spacecraft. Soyuz TMA-03M was launched on 21 December 2011 at 13:16 UTC\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ex31_9-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-ex31-9">\n         [9]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and docked on 23 December at 15:19 UTC.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10">\n        <a href="#cite_note-10">\n         [10]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Comet_Lovejoy_observation">\n        Comet Lovejoy observation\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Expedition_30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Comet Lovejoy observation">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <p>\n       On 21 December 2011, Expedition 30 commander\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dan_Burbank" title="Dan Burbank">\n        Dan Burbank\n       </a>\n       observed a pass of the comet\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/C/2011_W3" title="C/2011 W3">\n        C/2011 W3 Lovejoy\n       </a>\n       . The comet was initially thought to be in a destructive orbit around the sun, and passed within 140,000\xc2\xa0km (87,000\xc2\xa0mi) of the sun\'s surface.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11">\n        <a href="#cite_note-11">\n         [11]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       However, the comet ultimately survived its encounter with the sun.\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Spacewalk">\n        Spacewalk\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Expedition_30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Spacewalk">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <p>\n       On 16 February 2012, Russian crew members\n       <a href="/wiki/Oleg_Kononenko" title="Oleg Kononenko">\n        Oleg Kononenko\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/Anton_Shkaplerov" title="Anton Shkaplerov">\n        Anton Shkaplerov\n       </a>\n       performed the first\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Spacewalk" title="Spacewalk">\n        spacewalk\n       </a>\n       of 2012, moving one of the station\'s\n       <a href="/wiki/Strela_(crane)" title="Strela (crane)">\n        Strela\n       </a>\n       cranes from the\n       <a href="/wiki/Pirs_(ISS_module)" title="Pirs (ISS module)">\n        <i>\n         Pirs\n        </i>\n       </a>\n       module to the\n       <a href="/wiki/Poisk_(ISS_module)" title="Poisk (ISS module)">\n        <i>\n         Poisk\n        </i>\n       </a>\n       module. The astronauts also installed new debris shields and materials experiments on the exterior of the ISS.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12">\n        <a href="#cite_note-12">\n         [12]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Fiftieth_anniversary_of_Friendship_7">\n        Fiftieth anniversary of\n        <i>\n         Friendship 7\n        </i>\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Expedition_30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Fiftieth anniversary of Friendship 7">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <p>\n       On 20 February 2012, the ISS crew commemorated the fiftieth anniversary of\n       <a href="/wiki/John_Glenn" title="John Glenn">\n        John Glenn\n       </a>\n       \'s first orbital flight in the\n       <a href="/wiki/Project_Mercury" title="Project Mercury">\n        Project Mercury\n       </a>\n       spacecraft\n       <i>\n        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Friendship_7" title="Friendship 7">\n         Friendship 7\n        </a>\n       </i>\n       . The crew surprised the 90-year-old Glenn by speaking to him via video link while he was on-stage with\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NASA_Administrator" title="NASA Administrator">\n        NASA Administrator\n       </a>\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Charlie_Bolden" title="Charlie Bolden">\n        Charlie Bolden\n       </a>\n       at\n       <a href="/wiki/Ohio_State_University" title="Ohio State University">\n        Ohio State University\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13">\n        <a href="#cite_note-13">\n         [13]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Edoardo_Amaldi_ATV_docking">\n        <i>\n         Edoardo Amaldi\n        </i>\n        ATV docking\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Expedition_30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Edoardo Amaldi ATV docking">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <p>\n       The\n       <a href="/wiki/European_Space_Agency" title="European Space Agency">\n        European Space Agency\n       </a>\n       \'s third\n       <a href="/wiki/Automated_Transfer_Vehicle" title="Automated Transfer Vehicle">\n        Automated Transfer Vehicle\n       </a>\n       (ATV),\n       <a href="/wiki/Edoardo_Amaldi_ATV" title="Edoardo Amaldi ATV">\n        <i>\n         Edoardo Amaldi\n        </i>\n       </a>\n       , was launched on 23 March 2012, and docked successfully with the ISS on 28 March.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14">\n        <a href="#cite_note-14">\n         [14]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The robotic ATV carried around 6,595 kilograms (14,539\xc2\xa0lb) of propellants, water and dry cargo to the Expedition 30 crew, and also helped boost the station\'s altitude with its thrusters.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15">\n        <a href="#cite_note-15">\n         [15]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The ATV ultimately remained docked until September 2012, whereafter it deorbited and burned up in Earth\'s atmosphere as planned.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-OverOct4_16-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-OverOct4-16">\n         [16]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span   </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Expedition_30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Progress M-15M docking">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <p>\n       <a href="/wiki/Progress_M-15M" title="Progress M-15M">\n        Progress M-15M\n       </a>\n       , a Russian unmanned resupply spacecraft, was launched to the ISS from\n       <a href="/wiki/Baikonur_Cosmodrome" title="Baikonur Cosmodrome">\n        Baikonur Cosmodrome\n       </a>\n       on 20 April 2012. It successfully docked with the station on 22 April.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17">\n        <a href="#cite_note-17">\n         [17]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       In preparation for the spacecraft\'s arrival, its predecessor,\n       <a href="/wiki/Progress_M-14M" title="Progress M-14M">\n        Progress M-14M\n       </a>\n       , undocked from the ISS on 19 April, having been docked since 28 January.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18">\n        <a href="#cite_note-18">\n         [18]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Soyuz_TMA-22_departure">\n        Soyuz TMA-22 departure\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Expedition_30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Soyuz TMA-22 departure">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <p>\n       Expedition 30 ended on 27 April 2012 with the departure of Soyuz TMA-22 from the ISS, carrying astronauts Burbank, Shkaplerov and Ivanishin.
