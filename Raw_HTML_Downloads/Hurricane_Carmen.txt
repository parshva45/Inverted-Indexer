Carmen\n       </b>\n       was the most intense\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclone" title="Tropical cyclone">\n        tropical cyclone\n       </a>\n       of the\n       <a href="/wiki/1974_Atlantic_hurricane_season" title="1974 Atlantic hurricane season">\n        1974 Atlantic hurricane season\n       </a>\n       . A destructive and widespread storm, Carmen originated as a tropical disturbance that emerged from Africa toward the end of August. The disturbance traveled westward, spawning a tropical depression east of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Lesser_Antilles" title="Lesser Antilles">\n        Lesser Antilles\n       </a>\n       on August\xc2\xa029. The storm moved through the\n       <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Sea" title="Caribbean Sea">\n        Caribbean Sea\n       </a>\n       , and in an environment conducive to intensification, it quickly strengthened to its initial peak intensity as a Category\xc2\xa04 hurricane on the\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saffir%E2%80%93Simpson_Hurricane_Scale" title="Saffir\xe2\x80\x93Simpson Hurricane Scale">\n        Saffir\xe2\x80\x93Simpson Hurricane Scale\n       </a>\n       . Carmen moved ashore on the\n       <a href="/wiki/Yucat%C3%A1n_Peninsula" title="Yucat\xc3\xa1n Peninsula">\n        Yucat\xc3\xa1n Peninsula\n       </a>\n       , where, despite striking a sparsely populated region, it caused significant crop damage and killed several people. Before the storm\'s arrival, officials had set up several evacuation centers, and many residents had moved to higher ground.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Upon entering the\n       <a href="/wiki/Gulf_of_Mexico" title="Gulf of Mexico">\n        Gulf of Mexico\n       </a>\n       , Carmen turned northward and re-intensified as it approached the\n       <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">\n        United States\n       </a>\n       . Initially threatening the major city of\n       <a href="/wiki/New_Orleans" title="New Orleans">\n        New Orleans\n       </a>\n       , it veered westward and made landfall on the marshland of southern\n       <a href="/wiki/Louisiana" title="Louisiana">\n        Louisiana\n       </a>\n       , eventually dissipating over eastern\n       <a href="/wiki/Texas" title="Texas">\n        Texas\n       </a>\n       on September\xc2\xa010.\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclone_watches_and_warnings" title="Tropical cyclone watches and warnings">\n        Tropical cyclone watches and warnings\n       </a>\n       had been issued for the storm, and approximately 100,000 residents left their homes and sought shelter. Damage was lighter than first feared, but the sugar industry suffered substantial losses. Throughout its course, the hurricane killed 8 people and caused $162\xc2\xa0million in damage. Due to the severity of the storm, the name\n       <i>\n        Carmen\n       </i>\n       was\n       <a href="/wiki/List_of_retired_Atlantic_hurricane_names" title="List of retired Atlantic hurricane names">\n        retired\n       </a>\n       from the list of Atlantic tropical cyclone names.\n      </p>\n      <p>\n      </p>\n      <div class="toc" id="toc">\n       <div </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       The origins of Hurricane Carmen can be traced to a weather disturbance over\n       <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">\n        Africa\n       </a>\n       during the middle of August\xc2\xa01974. The disturbance moved slowly westward with little\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Convection_(meteorology)" title="Convection (meteorology)">\n        convective activity\n       </a>\n       initially, although upon entering the\n       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">\n        Atlantic Ocean\n       </a>\n       , it spawned a\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_wave" title="Tropical wave">\n        tropical wave\n       </a>\n       within the\n       <a href="/wiki/Intertropical_Convergence_Zone" title="Intertropical Convergence Zone">\n        Intertropical Convergence Zone\n       </a>\n       . The wave had intensified and broadened by August\xc2\xa025, and it eventually split into two components, the northernmost of which consolidated into an organized storm system.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-1">\n         [1]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Moving westward, the system developed into a tropical depression on August\xc2\xa029, more than 200\xc2\xa0mi (320\xc2\xa0km) east of\n       <a href="/wiki/Guadeloupe" title="Guadeloupe">\n        Guadeloupe\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HURDAT_2-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HURDAT-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Due to favorable\n       <a href="/wiki/Outflow_(meteorology)" title="Outflow (meteorology)">\n        outflow\n       </a>\n       from an\n       <a href="/wiki/Anticyclone" title="Anticyclone">\n        anticyclone\n       </a>\n       nearby, the depression gradually strengthened as it moved through the\n       <a href="/wiki/Lesser_Antilles" title="Lesser Antilles">\n        Lesser Antilles\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MWR_3-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-MWR-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       It attained tropical storm status on August 30, south of\n       <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">\n        Puerto Rico\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HURDAT_2-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HURDAT-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and was named Carmen by the\n       <a href="/wiki/National_Hurricane_Center" title="National Hurricane Center">\n        National Hurricane Center\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">\n        <a href="#cite_note-4">\n         [4]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       At first, the storm\'s proximity to\n       <a href="/wiki/Hispaniola" title="Hispaniola">\n        Hispaniola\n       </a>\n       prevented further strengthening,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5">\n        <a href="#cite_note-5">\n         [5]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       but by August\xc2\xa031, it had managed to intensify into a Category\xc2\xa01 hurricane on the\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saffir%E2%80%93Simpson_Hurricane_Scale" title="Saffir\xe2\x80\x93Simpson Hurricane Scale">\n        Saffir\xe2\x80\x93Simpson Hurricane Scale\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HURDAT_2-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HURDAT-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       As Carmen passed south of\n       <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">\n        Jamaica\n       </a>\n       , an\n       <a href="/wiki/Eye_(cyclone)" title="Eye (cyclone)">\n        eye feature\n       </a>\n       briefly appeared.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6">\n        <a href="#cite_note-6">\n         [6]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       On September\xc2\xa01, the hurricane began to\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rapid_deepening" title="Rapid deepening">\n        rapidly deepen\n       </a>\n       over warm waters of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Sea" title="Caribbean Sea">\n        Caribbean Sea\n       </a>\n       ; by 1800\n       <a href="/wiki/Coordinated_Universal_Time" title="Coordinated Universal Time">\n        Coordinated Universal Time\n       </a>\n       (UTC), it had strengthened to Category\xc2\xa04 intensity.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HURDAT_2-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HURDAT-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Continuing westward, the storm passed north of\n       <a href="/wiki/Swan_Islands,_Honduras" title="Swan Islands, Honduras">\n        Swan Island\n       </a>\n       later that day.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MWR_3-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-MWR-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Early on September\xc2\xa02, a\n       <a href="/wiki/Eyewall_replacement_cycle" title="Eyewall replacement cycle">\n        double eyewall\n       </a>\n       appeared on satellite imagery.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7">\n        <a href="#cite_note-7">\n         [7]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Carmen\'s forward movement gradually slowed as the storm took a\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/West_by_north" title="West by north">\n        west by north\n       </a>\n       direction,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8">\n        <a href="#cite_note-8">\n         [8]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and it reached its initial peak intensity with\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Maximum_sustained_winds" title="Maximum sustained winds">\n        maximum sustained winds\n       </a>\n       of 150\xc2\xa0mph (240\xc2\xa0km/h), accompanied by a central\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Barometric_pressure" title="Barometric pressure">\n        barometric pressure\n       </a>\n       of 928\n       <a href="/wiki/Bar_(unit)" title="Bar (unit)">\n        mbar\n       </a>\n       (hPa; 27.4\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Inches_of_mercury" title="Inches of mercury">\n        inHg\n       </a>\n       ).\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HURDAT_2-4">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HURDAT-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Atmospheric steering currents became increasingly weaker, and Carmen slowed to a drift.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9">\n        <a href="#cite_note-9">\n         [9]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Later on September\xc2\xa02, the hurricane made\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Landfall_(meteorology)" title="Landfall (meteorology)">\n        landfall\n       </a>\n       on the\n       <a href="/wiki/Yucat%C3%A1n_Peninsula" title="Yucat\xc3\xa1n Peninsula">\n        Yucat\xc3\xa1n Peninsula\n       </a>\n       ; its northern jog spared\n       <a href="/wiki/Belize_City" title="Belize City">\n        Belize City\n       </a>\n       from a direct hit. The storm\'s center passed a few miles north of\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chetumal,_Quintana_Roo" title="Chetumal, Quintana Roo">\n        Chetumal\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Quintana_Roo" title="Quintana Roo">\n        Quintana Roo\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HURDAT_2-5">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HURDAT-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       The cyclone drifted inland, deteriorating to a tropical storm on September\xc2\xa03.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HURDAT_2-6">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HURDAT-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       About a day later, Carmen emerged into the\n       <a href="/wiki/Gulf_of_Mexico" title="Gulf of Mexico">\n        Gulf of Mexico\n       </a>\n       , where it nearly stalled. Turning northward, the storm regained hurricane strength on September\xc2\xa05.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HURDAT_2-7">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HURDAT-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Carmen continued to strengthen and accelerated northward towards the\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/United_States_Gulf_Coast" title="United States Gulf Coast">\n        United States Gulf Coast\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MWR_3-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-MWR-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       reaching a forward speed of 12\xc2\xa0mph (19\xc2\xa0km/h); at 0000\xc2\xa0UTC on September\xc2\xa07, it once again became a Category\xc2\xa03 major hurricane.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HURDAT_2-8">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HURDAT-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The storm reached its second peak intensity while located south of\n       <a href="/wiki/Louisiana" title="Louisiana">\n        Louisiana\n       </a>\n       ; although the wind speeds were identical to that of its initial peak, the barometric pressure was slightly higher. Carmen weakened and veered westward before landfall, ultimately striking south-central Louisiana. After moving ashore, the hurricane quickly lost strength and late on September\xc2\xa09 degenerated into a tropical depression. The depression moved westward and soon dissipated over eastern\n       <a href="/wiki/Texas" title="Texas">\n        Texas\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HURDAT_2-9">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HURDAT-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Preparations">\n        Preparations\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Hurricane_Carmen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Preparations">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <div class="thumb tright">\n       <div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;">\n        <a class="image"       <div class="thumbcaption">\n         <div class="magnify">\n          <a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Hurricane_Carmen_near_United_States_Landfall_1974.jpg" title="Enlarge">\n          </a>\n         </div>\n         Hurricane Carmen in the Gulf of Mexico near landfall in Louisiana\n        </div>\n       </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       Initial reactions to the approaching hurricane in the Yucat\xc3\xa1n Peninsula were regarded as calm by the United States media. Mexican officials declared an emergency alert by September\xc2\xa02, although they did not advise any evacuations. Meteorologists in the United States urged those living near the coast to move inland immediately.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TVA1_10-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-TVA1-10">\n         [10]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Fearing significant loss of life and property, the\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Red_Cross" title="Red Cross">\n        Red Cross\n       </a>\n       began preparations for the approaching hurricane in Belize.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TVA1_10-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-TVA1-10">\n         [10]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The following day, the\n       <a href="/wiki/Mexican_Army" title="Mexican Army">\n        Mexican Army\n       </a>\n       rushed to set up emergency operation centers and shelters in five cities. Mobile communication units and relief teams were prepared for deployment following the storm\'s passage.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BCT1_11-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-BCT1-11">\n         [11]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Many of the nearly 35,000\xc2\xa0residents in and around the city of Chetumal evacuated to higher ground.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SHT1_12-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-SHT1-12">\n         [12]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Although it initially threatened the United States city of\n       <a href="/wiki/New_Orleans" title="New Orleans">\n        New Orleans\n       </a>\n       , the hurricane turned west prior to making landfall and spared the area from severe damage.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MWR_3-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-MWR-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Contrary to its actual path, forecasters predicted the hurricane to execute an eastward swerve toward\n       <a href="/wiki/Florida" title="Florida">\n        Florida\n       </a>\n       . Had the cyclone instead continued northward and traveled over\n       <a href="/wiki/Lake_Pontchartrain" title="Lake Pontchartrain">\n        Lake Pontchartrain\n       </a>\n       , low-lying areas could have suffered "catastrophic" flooding.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LA_preps_13-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-LA_preps-13">\n         [13]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Over 100,000\xc2\xa0residents of the\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/U.S._Gulf_Coast" title="U.S. Gulf Coast">\n        Gulf Coast\n       </a>\n       , mostly in\n       <a href="/wiki/Louisiana" title="Louisiana">\n        Louisiana\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/Mississippi" title="Mississippi">\n        Mississippi\n       </a>\n       , evacuated in advance of the hurricane,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14">\n        <a href="#cite_note-14">\n         [14]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       causing heavy congestion on highways.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LA_preps_13-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-LA_preps-13">\n         [13]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       About 60,000\xc2\xa0people sought shelter in facilities across the New Orleans region, according to Red Cross officials.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15">\n        <a href="#cite_note-15">\n         [15]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hurricane_warning" title="Hurricane warning">\n        Hurricane warnings\n       </a>\n       were issued along the coast, while\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coast_Guard" title="Coast Guard">\n        Coast Guard\n       </a>\n       personnel went door-to-door on\n       <a href="/wiki/Grand_Isle,_Louisiana" title="Grand Isle, Louisiana">\n        Grand Isle\n       </a>\n       urging residents to leave the area. From there through the coast of southwestern Florida, small craft were advised to remain near shore due to rough seas.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16">\n        <a href="#cite_note-16">\n         [16]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Offshore, workers were removed from oil rigs.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17">\n        <a href="#cite_note-17">\n         [17]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Many Mississippi citizens, having experienced the destruction of\n       <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Camille" title="Hurricane Camille">\n        Hurricane Camille\n       </a>\n       just five years earlier, quickly left their coastal homes.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18">\n        <a href="#cite_note-18">\n         [18]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Impact">\n        Impact\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Hurricane_Carmen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Impact">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       As a tropical depression and storm, Carmen produced moderate rainfall across\n       <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">\n        Puerto Rico\n       </a>\n       and the northern Lesser Antilles, peaking at 5.91\xc2\xa0in (150\xc2\xa0mm) in\n       <a href="/wiki/Cayey,_Puerto_Rico" title="Cayey, Puerto Rico">\n        Jajome Alto, Puerto Rico\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HPC_19-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HPC-19">\n         [19]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The storm spawned a tornado on Puerto Rico and triggered flash flooding, which collectively left over $2\xc2\xa0million in damage.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MWR_3-4">\n        <a href="#cite_note-MWR-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Winds approaching\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gale_force" title="Gale force">\n        gale force\n       </a>\n       affected several islands.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20">\n        <a href="#cite_note-20">\n         [20]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Heavy rain fell on\n       <a href="/wiki/Hispaniola" title="Hispaniola">\n        Hispaniola\n       </a>\n       as the storm progressed westward,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21">\n        <a href="#cite_note-21">\n         [21]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and on\n       <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">\n        Jamaica\n       </a>\n       , the storm caused three drownings.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-22">\n        <a href="#cite_note-22">\n         [22]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       High winds and heavy rainfall were reported there and in\n       <a href="/wiki/Cuba" title="Cuba">\n        Cuba\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-23">\n        <a href="#cite_note-23">\n         [23]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The hurricane damaged local reefs on the north shore of Jamaica during its passage.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-24">\n        <a href="#cite_note-24">\n         [24]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Following the storm, the name\n       <i>\n        Carmen\n       </i>\n       was\n       <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclone_naming#Retirement" title="Tropical cyclone naming">\n        retired\n       </a>\n       from the annually rotating list of hurricane names.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-25">\n        <a href="#cite_note-25">\n         [25]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       However, due to a change in the naming scheme in 1979, it was not replaced by any particular name.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-26">\n        <a href="#cite_note-26">\n         [26]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span id="Yucat\xc3\xa1n_Peninsula">\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Yucat.C3.A1n_Peninsula">\n        Yucat\xc3\xa1n Peninsula\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Hurricane_Carmen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Yucat\xc3\xa1n Peninsula">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <div class="thumb tright">\n       <div class="thumbinner" style="width:202px;">\n        <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Hurricane_Carmen_at_landfall_in_the_Yucat%C3%A1n_(radar_image).jpg">\n         <img alt="Radar image of Hurricane Carmen" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="247" data-file-width="240" height="206" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/50/Hurricane_Carmen_at_landfall_in_the_Yucat%C3%A1n_%28radar_image%29.jpg/200px-Hurricane_Carmen_at_landfall_in_the_Yucat%C3%A1n_%28radar_image%29.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/50/Hurricane_Carmen_at_landfall_in_the_Yucat%C3%A1n_%28radar_image%29.jpg 1.5x" width="200"/>\n        </a>\n        <div class="thumbcaption">\n         <div class="magnify">\n          <a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Hurricane_Carmen_at_landfall_in_the_Yucat%C3%A1n_(radar_image).jpg" title="Enlarge">\n          </a>\n         </div>\n         Radar image of Hurricane Carmen near landfall on September 2\n        </div>\n       </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       Although Carmen made landfall as a powerful Category\xc2\xa04 hurricane, it caused significantly less damage than anticipated because it struck a sparsely populated region. However, torrential rainfall from the storm inundated farmland across the region, ruining rice crops. The fishing industry also sustained major losses. Communication with the hardest hit regions was lost following Carmen\'s passage; however, early reports stated that at least five people were injured.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BCT1_11-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-BCT1-11">\n         [11]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Several days later, officials in Mexico confirmed that three people had been killed by the storm. The city of Chetumal was described as a "disaster", and hundreds of people were left homeless.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-27">\n        <a href="#cite_note-27">\n         [27]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       More than 5,000\xc2\xa0people in the city lost their belongings as a result of the storm. Officials in the area estimated that damage in Chetumal alone reached $8\xc2\xa0million (1974\xc2\xa0USD).\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TMG1_28-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-TMG1-28">\n         [28]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Throughout the Yucat\xc3\xa1n Peninsula, Hurricane Carmen claimed four lives and wrought $10\xc2\xa0million (1974\xc2\xa0USD) in damage.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-29">\n        <a href="#cite_note-29">\n         [29]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Following Carmen\'s passage, officials feared the worst for an area of 1,000\xc2\xa0mi\n       <sup>\n        2\n       </sup>\n       (2,590\xc2\xa0km\n       <sup>\n        2\n       </sup>\n       ) where communication was lost in Belize. A reconnaissance task force was sent out from Belize City the day after Carmen made landfall to assist any residents stranded by the storm.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SHT2_30-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-SHT2-30">\n         [30]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       One person was killed off the coast of Belize after being washed off his boat by large swells produced by Carmen. Three other fishermen were listed as missing following similar incidents. Thousands of people moved from coastal areas inland to escape the storm. Crop damage in the country was reportedly severe.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-31">\n        <a href="#cite_note-31">\n         [31]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h3>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="United_States">\n        United States\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Hurricane_Carmen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: United States">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h3>\n      <p>\n       Carmen dropped moderate rainfall along its path, though the heaviest rainfall occurred well to the east of the storm\'s center, in southern\n       <a href="/wiki/Alabama" title="Alabama">\n        Alabama\n       </a>\n       and the northern\n       <a href="/wiki/Florida_Panhandle" title="Florida Panhandle">\n        Florida Panhandle\n       </a>\n       . Precipitation peaked at over 13\xc2\xa0in (330\xc2\xa0mm) in\n       <a href="/wiki/Atmore,_Alabama" title="Atmore, Alabama">\n        Atmore, Alabama\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HPC_19-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HPC-19">\n         [19]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Winds gusted up to 86\xc2\xa0mph (138\xc2\xa0km/h), and along the coast tides ran as high as 6\xc2\xa0ft (1.8\xc2\xa0m) above normal.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MWR_3-5">\n        <a href="#cite_note-MWR-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Over northwestern Louisiana, winds ranged from 40 to 45\xc2\xa0mph (64 to 72\xc2\xa0km/h) and brought down several trees.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-32">\n        <a href="#cite_note-32">\n         [32]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       In New Orleans, despite wind gusts to 72\xc2\xa0mph (116\xc2\xa0km/h), minimal damage was reported.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-33">\n        <a href="#cite_note-33">\n         [33]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The hurricane\'s effects in\n       <a href="/wiki/Baton_Rouge,_Louisiana" title="Baton Rouge, Louisiana">\n        Baton Rouge\n       </a>\n       were confined to strewn debris and a few downed trees.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-34">\n        <a href="#cite_note-34">\n         [34]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Because Carmen moved ashore over uninhabited marshland, it caused far less damage than initially feared.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-35">\n        <a href="#cite_note-35">\n         [35]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Nonetheless, tidal flooding from the Gulf of Mexico and coastal bodies of water was severe. Freshwater flooding was less extreme. In total, the storm inundated 2,380,500 acres (963,400\xc2\xa0ha) of land in Louisiana, including 742,300 acres (300,400\xc2\xa0ha) in\n       <a href="/wiki/Terrebonne_Parish,_Louisiana" title="Terrebonne Parish, Louisiana">\n        Terrebonne Parish\n       </a>\n       and 590,000 acres (240,000\xc2\xa0ha) in\n       <a href="/wiki/Plaquemines_Parish,_Louisiana" title="Plaquemines Parish, Louisiana">\n        Plaquemines Parish\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-phase_36-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-phase-36">\n         [36]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-37">\n        <a href="#cite_note-37">\n         [37]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <div class="thumb tleft">\n       <div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;">\n        <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Carmen_1974_rainfall.png">\n         <img alt="Map of the southeastern United States showing rainfall amounts from Hurricane Carmen" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="776" data-file-width="1061" height="161" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/08/Carmen_1974_rainfall.png/220px-Carmen_1974_rainfall.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/08/Carmen_1974_rainfall.png/330px-Carmen_1974_rainfall.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/08/Carmen_1974_rainfall.png/440px-Carmen_1974_rainfall.png 2x" width="220"/>\n        </a>\n        <div class="thumbcaption">\n         <div class="magnify">\n          <a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Carmen_1974_rainfall.png" title="Enlarge">\n          </a>\n         </div>\n         Rainfall map of the\n         <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gulf_Coast" title="Gulf Coast">\n          Gulf Coast\n         </a>\n        </div>\n       </div>\n      </div>\n      <p>\n       The storm\'s greatest impact was the loss of sugar cane crops in Louisiana. An estimated 308,000 acres (125,000\xc2\xa0ha) of sugar cane in 16\xc2\xa0parishes was damaged, and about 20\xc2\xa0percent was completely ruined.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-38">\n        <a href="#cite_note-38">\n         [38]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       After a tour of the affected area, then-\n       <a href="/wiki/List_of_Governors_of_Louisiana" title="List of Governors of Louisiana">\n        Governor\n       </a>\n       <a href="/wiki/Edwin_Edwards" title="Edwin Edwards">\n        Edwin Edwards\n       </a>\n       estimated crop damage alone at $400\xc2\xa0million,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Beaver_39-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-Beaver-39">\n         [39]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       although a more recent estimate placed total agricultural damage from the hurricane at $74\xc2\xa0million.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-phase_36-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-phase-36">\n         [36]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The sugar cane crop was crucial to the country\'s sugar supplies, rendering the losses "doubly bad",\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Beaver_39-1">\n        <a href="#cite_note-Beaver-39">\n         [39]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and sugar\n       <a href="/wiki/Futures_contract" title="Futures contract">\n        futures\n       </a>\n       rose drastically after the storm.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-40">\n        <a href="#cite_note-40">\n         [40]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Other crops damaged by Carmen included soybeans, rice, and cotton.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-phase_36-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-phase-36">\n         [36]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       Tidal action along the coast affected the balance of salinity in coastal marshes and water bodies. The sudden intrusion of saltwater stressed delicate plants. Fish, shrimp and oysters also suffered the ecological effects of Hurricane Carmen. Flooding on land caused some wildlife to drown. Several parks in Louisiana sustained damage, either from flooding or high winds; losses to\n       <a href="/wiki/Grand_Isle,_Louisiana" title="Grand Isle, Louisiana">\n        Grand Island\n       </a>\n       State Park in particular totaled $114,600. The oil and gas industry was also affected, and its estimated $24.7\xc2\xa0million in losses resulted mainly from damage to equipment and offshore facilities. The storm diminished oil production by 1.4 million barrels when it shut down operations for 24 to 48 hours at various locations.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-phase_36-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-phase-36">\n         [36]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Over 60,000\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Electric_cooperative" title="Electric cooperative">\n        electric cooperative\n       </a>\n       customers lost power.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-41">\n        <a href="#cite_note-41">\n         [41]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <p>\n       The hurricane caused two fatalities in Louisiana: a utility repairman who was electrocuted while working on power lines damaged by strong winds,\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Beaver_39-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-Beaver-39">\n         [39]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       and a motorist who was involved in a storm-related traffic accident.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-42">\n        <a href="#cite_note-42">\n         [42]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Total monetary losses in the state was estimated $150 million.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MWR_3-6">\n        <a href="#cite_note-MWR-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Overall, the hurricane spawned four confirmed tornadoes.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-43">\n        <a href="#cite_note-43">\n         [43]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       One touched down near\n       <a href="/wiki/Brandon,_Mississippi" title="Brandon, Mississippi">\n        Brandon, Mississippi\n       </a>\n       , destroying a barn and causing other damage.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-44">\n        <a href="#cite_note-44">\n         [44]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Another struck\n       <a href="/wiki/Kaplan,_Louisiana" title="Kaplan, Louisiana">\n        Kaplan, Louisiana\n       </a>\n       , injuring one person.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-45">\n        <a href="#cite_note-45">\n         [45]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The storm\'s effects in Mississippi were described as minimal and were mainly confined to minor traffic accidents during bouts of heavy precipitation.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-46">\n        <a href="#cite_note-46">\n         [46]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Light to moderate rainfall from the storm extended as far east as Florida and Georgia and as far west as Oklahoma and Texas.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HPC_19-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-HPC-19">\n         [19]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="See_also">\n
