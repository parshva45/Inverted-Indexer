<span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sea_spray&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Composition">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       <a href="/wiki/Sea_salt_aerosol" title="Sea salt aerosol">\n        Sea salt aerosol\n       </a>\n       (SSA) contains both inorganic salts and organic matter from the ocean.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">\n        <a href="#cite_note-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       It is thought that the amount of organic matter transferred to SSA depends on microbiological activity.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       The organic matter in sea spray can contain\n       <a href="/wiki/Dissolved_organic_carbon" title="Dissolved organic carbon">\n        dissolved organic matter\n       </a>\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">\n        <a href="#cite_note-4">\n         [4]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       or even microbes themselves, like bacteria and viruses.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5">\n        <a href="#cite_note-5">\n         [5]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Effects">\n        Effects\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sea_spray&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Effects">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       Salt spray is largely responsible for\n       <a href="/wiki/Corrosion" title="Corrosion">\n        corrosion\n       </a>\n       of metallic objects near the coastline, as the salts accelerate the corrosion process in the presence of abundant atmospheric\n       <a href="/wiki/Oxygen" title="Oxygen">\n        oxygen\n       </a>\n       and moisture. Salts do not dissolve in air directly, but are suspended as fine\n       <a href="/wiki/Particulates" title="Particulates">\n        particulates\n       </a>\n       , or dissolved in microscopic airborne water droplets.\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="Chemical_resistance">\n        Chemical resistance\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sea_spray&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Chemical resistance">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <p>\n       The\n       <a href="/wiki/Salt_spray_test" title="Salt spray test">\n        salt spray test\n       </a>\n       is the notable measure of material endurance, particularly if the material will be used outdoors and must perform in a\n       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Mechanical_load_bearing&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Mechanical load bearing (page does not exist)">\n        mechanical load bearing\n       </a>\n       or otherwise critical role. These results are often of great interest to the\n       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marine_industry" title="Marine industry">\n        marine industries\n       </a>\n       , whose products may suffer extreme acceleration of corrosion and subsequent failure due to the salt water environment.\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="See_also">\n        See also\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         [\n        </span>\n        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sea_spray&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: See also">\n         edit\n        </a>\n        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">\n         ]\n        </span>\n       </span>\n      </h2>\n      <div class="div-col columns column-count column-count-2" style="-moz-column-count: 2;
