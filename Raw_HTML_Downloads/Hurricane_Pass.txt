<p>\n       <b>\n        Hurricane Pass\n       </b>\n       is a pedestrian\n       <a href="/wiki/Mountain_pass" title="Mountain pass">\n        mountain pass\n       </a>\n       located in the\n       <a href="/wiki/Teton_Range" title="Teton Range">\n        Teton Range\n       </a>\n       ,\n       <a href="/wiki/Grand_Teton_National_Park" title="Grand Teton National Park">\n        Grand Teton National Park\n       </a>\n       , in the\n       <a href="/wiki/U.S._state" title="U.S. state">\n        U.S. state\n       </a>\n       of\n       <a href="/wiki/Wyoming" title="Wyoming">\n        Wyoming\n       </a>\n       .\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-topo_2-0">\n        <a href="#cite_note-topo-2">\n         [2]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Situated at approximately 10,338\xc2\xa0ft (3,151\xc2\xa0m) above sea level, the pass can be accessed from the south by way of the\n       <a href="/wiki/Teton_Crest_Trail" title="Teton Crest Trail">\n        Teton Crest Trail\n       </a>\n       or from the north via the\n       <a href="/wiki/South_Fork_Cascade_Canyon_Trail" title="South Fork Cascade Canyon Trail">\n        South Fork Cascade Canyon Trail\n       </a>\n       . From\n       <a href="/wiki/Jenny_Lake" title="Jenny Lake">\n        Jenny Lake\n       </a>\n       the roundtrip hike is 24.8\xc2\xa0mi (39.9\xc2\xa0km) with a 4,500\xc2\xa0ft (1,400\xc2\xa0m) elevation gain.\n       <a href="/wiki/Schoolroom_Glacier" title="Schoolroom Glacier">\n        Schoolroom Glacier\n       </a>\n       is 100\xc2\xa0yd (91\xc2\xa0m) from the pass.\n       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">\n        <a href="#cite_note-3">\n         [3]\n        </a>\n       </sup>\n       Views of the west slopes of several of the highest Teton Range peaks as well as\n       <a href="/wiki/Alaska_Basin" title="Alaska Basin">\n        Alaska Basin\n       </a>\n       and\n       <a href="/wiki/Battleship_Mountain" title="Battleship Mountain">\n        Battleship Mountain\n       </a>\n       in\n       <a href="/wiki/Caribou-Targhee_National_Forest" title="Caribou-Targhee National Forest">\n        Caribou-Targhee National Forest\n       </a>\n       are available at the pass.\n      </p>\n      <h2>\n       <span class="mw-headline" id="References">\n        References\n       </span>\n       <span class="mw-editsection">\n        <span
