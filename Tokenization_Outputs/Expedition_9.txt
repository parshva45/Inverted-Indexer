spacewalks edit the expedition 9 crew conducted four spacewalks during its stay at the international space station the four spacewalks were devoted to iss maintenance and assembly all four were based out of the pirs docking compartment and used russian orlan spacesuits before these four extravehicular activities evas 52 spacewalks had been performed at the iss with 27 based out of the station
