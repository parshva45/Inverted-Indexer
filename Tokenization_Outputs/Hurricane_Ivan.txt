fourth major hurricane of the active 2004 atlantic hurricane season ivan formed in early september and reached category 5 strength on the saffir simpson hurricane scale ivan caused catastrophic damage subtropical atlantic and regenerated into a tropical cyclone which then moved across florida and the gulf of mexico into louisiana and texas causing minimal damage ivan caused an estimated in damages
