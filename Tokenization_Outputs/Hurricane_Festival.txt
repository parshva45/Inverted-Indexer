hurricane is a music festival that takes place in near bremen germany usually every june the festival and its guests are generally associated with the alternative part of mainstream music although the festival has been becoming more and more mainstream over the years the southside festival often referred to as the sister of hurricane takes place on the same three days in the very south of germany contrary to hurricane which is in the north together they are a twin festival since they are on the same days and usually have the same lineup with only few exceptions contents 1 history 2 past lineups 3 see also 4 references 5 external links history edit in 1973 the first festival was held in the place where hurricane takes place it was called es rockt in der heide at that time literally translated rocking in the heath and was attended by 52,000 people at the first rider open air in 1977 fans caused damage of more than a million marks when riots broke out because only five of the announced 23 bands could huge scandal was uncovered few days later forcing the organizing management to discontinue their festivals in in 1997 rock returned to in the form of the hurricane festival with numerous bands performing in front of 20,000 visitors it has taken place on the eichenring a motorcycle sand racetrack ever since audience size peaked in 2008 with about 75,000 guests from 2002 to 2006 between 40,000 and 60,000 people came in 2003 the festival was extended from two to three days in 2007 60,000 and in 2008 70.000 tickets were sold 1 in 2004 david bowie suffered from chest
