expeditions expedition 42 expedition 44 expedition 43 was the 43rd expedition to the international space station it commenced on 11 march 2015 with the undocking of soyuz tma 14m returning the crew of expedition 42 to earth and ended with the departure of soyuz tma 15m on 11 june 2015 the expedition 43 spend an extra four weeks due to a delay caused by a re supply spacecraft issue and the extra time was called a bonus month by the crew 1 on june 8 2015 iss adjusted its orbit to allow a bigger distance to a piece of orbital space debris 2 this expedition also used the isspresso machine and
