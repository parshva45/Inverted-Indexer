written in 2013 however several previously unreleased songs were recorded and included in the final release wish was written in 2001 and intended to be the final track on the 2002 album stanley 2010 release of smoke mirrors while angeline was written prior to the sessions 2 17 wish central park clarity and angeline were originally slated for shelved solo album paper cuts initially set for a 2013 release 18 hourglass began in the early 1990s as an instrumental piece by producer jude cole and film score composer james newton howard before resurfacing during the out of the wasteland
