hurricanes is a song by pull tiger tail released as their fourth single and their last under the b unique records label the physical format of the single was released on june 18 on cd 7 gatefold 7 vinyl whereas the digital single was made available from june 11 the song debuted at number 2 in the uk indie chart an acoustic version of the track was included on the nme essential bands collection
