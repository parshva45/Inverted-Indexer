of december january and february in the southern hemisphere 4 5 under meteorological definitions all seasons are arbitrarily set to start at the beginning of a calendar month and end at the end of a month 4 this meteorological definition of summer also aligns with the commonly viewed notion of summer as the season with the longest and warmest days of the year in which daylight predominates the meteorological reckoning of seasons is used in australia austria denmark the former soviet union and japan it is also used by many in the united kingdom in ireland the summer months according to the
