customs the first or paternal family name is duque and the second or maternal family name is duque pedro duque duque born 14 march 1963 is a spanish astronaut and a veteran of two space missions duque earned a degree in aeronautical engineering from the universidad de madrid upm in 1986 he worked for gmv and for the european space agency esa for six years before being selected as an astronaut candidate in 1992 duque underwent training in both russia and the united states his first spaceflight was as a mission specialist aboard space shuttle mission sts 95 during which duque supervised esa experimental modules in october 2003 duque visited the international space station for several days during a crew changeover the scientific program of this visit was called by esa spain cervantes he worked at the upm in the escuela superior de ingenieros 1 he used to work at deimos imaging currently he is back as an astronaut of esa and leads the flight operations office near munich see also edit
