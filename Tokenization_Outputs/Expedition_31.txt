expedition 30 expedition 32 expedition 31 was the 31st long duration expedition to the international space station iss it began on 27 april 2012 with the departure from the iss of the soyuz tma 22 spacecraft which returned the expedition 30 crew to earth 1 the expedition ended on 1 july 2012 when crew members oleg kononenko kuipers and don pettit departed from the iss aboard soyuz tma 03m
